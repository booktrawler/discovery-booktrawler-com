FAQ

What is The Long Middle?

A series of 10 short books for navigating the second half of life. Each book explores a single theme — identity, purpose, intimacy, mortality, legacy — in a focused, readable format. All are free PDF downloads.

How do I download the books?

Click any "Read More" button to visit a book's page, then click the "Download Free PDF" button. No email required.

Who wrote these books?

The Long Middle is published by tom/rhodes. Each book is written with care and precision, designed to deliver maximum insight in minimum time.

How long does each book take to read?

Each book is 6,000–8,200 words, roughly 11 chapters. Most readers finish one in a single sitting.
