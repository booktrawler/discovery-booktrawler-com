Romance Novels for Fans of 'You've Got Mail' & 'When Harry Met Sally'

Savor the wit, charm, and second chances in these modern romances that echo the classics.

Who these books are for

  • Fans of classic romantic comedies
  • Readers seeking modern, witty romance
  • Anyone looking for books with second chance love stories

Start here

If you only read one book about charming, modern romances, choose Abby Jimenez's 'The Night We Met (Deluxe Edition)' for its humor, heart, and 'meet-cute' magic.

Fans of classic romantic comedies like 'You've Got Mail' and 'When Harry Met Sally' often crave modern stories with the same wit, charm, and second-chance romance. This curated collection of 12 books delivers the humor, heart, and meet-cutes you've come to adore in those beloved films. From charming, witty protagonists to laugh-out-loud dialogue and satisfying happily-ever-afters, these contemporary romances are sure to delight readers seeking the perfect blend of humor and emotion in their love stories.

The collection

  1. The Kiss Quotient

    Helen Hoang

    Stella Lane, a brilliant math whiz with Asperger's, seeks to master the art of romance with the help of handsome escort Michael Phan. As their unconventional lessons progress from French kissing to more, Stella finds herself craving not just his expertise but the very feelings he evokes. A delightful, sexy, and emotionally resonant tale about embracing the unpredictability of love.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. Book Lovers

    Emily Henry

    Experience the delightful banter, witty characters, and charming romance in Emily Henry's instant #1 New York Times bestseller Book Lovers. When literary rival Nora Stephens clashes with brooding editor Charlie in a quaint small town, their contentious history threatens to overshadow a budding connection. As they're repeatedly thrown together, Nora and Charlie must confront the carefully crafted stories of their own lives, and the very possibility of love and transformation. A heartwarming, insightful tale that will captivate book lovers and anyone seeking a spellbinding summer romance.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. The Love Hypothesis

    Ali Hazelwood

    In this addictive and charming romantic comedy, a third-year Ph.D. candidate concocts a fake relationship with her nemesis, a hotshot professor, to appease her best friend's belief in love. But as their charade ignites into a real attraction, Olive's carefully calculated theories on romance are thrown into chaos. With its witty banter, steamy chemistry, and a dash of science, The Love Hypothesis is a delightful read that will keep you hooked until the very end.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. Love You More A Novel

    Emily Giffin

    In this emotionally charged novel, Emily Giffin masterfully weaves together past and present as Billie's seemingly perfect life is upended by an urgent call from her first love, Mick. Jolted back to her Wisconsin roots, Billie must confront the choices that led her away and where her heart truly lies, all while navigating the fragility of love and its power to transform everything. Giffin's gripping tale of love, loss, and second chances makes for an unforgettable read.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. Dark Matter A Novel

    Blake Crouch

    Blending sci-fi and psychological suspense, Blake Crouch's "Dark Matter" is a mind-bending thriller about an ordinary man who wakes up in a reality that's not his own, forcing him to confront choices, alternate paths, and what he'll risk to reclaim his family and life as he knows it. With echoes of Charlie Kaufman's "Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind," this gripping page-turner will leave you questioning the nature of reality.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. Love by the Book A Novel

    Jessica George

    In this heartfelt novel, a struggling author and a guarded kindergarten teacher form an unexpected bond, illuminating the transformative power of female friendship. Through their relationships and the secrets they harbor, Simone and Remy learn to open up and discover that platonic love can be just as life-changing as romantic love. A witty, poignant exploration of the resilience of close ties, "Love by the Book" is a must-read for anyone who's ever found solace and joy in a cherished friendship.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. This Story Might Save Your Life A Novel

    Tiffany Crum

    "Get ready for a heart-pounding thrill ride! Tiffany Crum weaves a gripping tale of best friends Benny and Joy, podcast hosts who share harrowing survival stories. But when Joy vanishes, Benny is accused of murder, and a sinister web of secrets from their past threatens to unravel their empire. A riveting mystery, tender romance, and pulse-defining suspense make this instant NYT bestseller a must-read for fans of pulse-pounding page-turners."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. Choose Newsroom PDX, #1

    L.J. Breedlove

    In this gripping new-adult suspense novel, J.J.'s journalistic dreams collide with his family's expectations, forcing him to confront the tough choices that shape both his future and Portland's complex reality. A story of identity, loyalty, and the power of the truth, Choose is a raw, unflinching portrayal of the city's politics and the high stakes of following one's convictions.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. Book Boyfriend The brand new charming and hilarious romcom from bestselling author

    Lucy Vine

    Lucy Vine's signature blend of witty humor, relatable characters, and heartfelt romance makes for an irresistible read.

    For: Fans of romantic comedies, book lovers, and anyone seeking an entertaining, feel-good story.

    Strengths: Amusing dialogue, engaging characters, and a charming premise that cleverly explores the power of literature in relationships.

    Skip if: Some readers might find the sisters' obsessive focus on fictional characters a bit far-fetched or over-the-top.

    Comparable: Compared to other romcoms in the collection, Book Boyfriend stands out for its unique premise that celebrates the joy of reading and the escapism it provides.

    “By turns proper laugh-out-loud funny and heart in the mouth moving, Book Boyfriend is Lucy Vine at the absolute top of her game! Clear your weekend because you”

  10. Before I Let Go

    Kennedy Ryan

    Before I Let Go is a moving, thought-provoking exploration of love, loss, and second chances that will resonate deeply with readers.

    For: Fans of emotional, character-driven romance and those seeking a nuanced portrayal of relationships and personal growth.

    Strengths: Kennedy Ryan masterfully crafts complex, relatable characters, weaving a compelling narrative that tackles heavy themes with sensitivity and authenticity.

    Skip if: The story's introspective, sometimes melancholic tone may not appeal to readers seeking lighter, more upbeat fare.

    Comparable: Readers who appreciate Colleen Hoover's emotional depth and character-driven storytelling will find similar resonance in Kennedy Ryan's Before I Let Go.

    “Before I Let Go takes readers on an emotional rollercoaster.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  11. Say You'll Remember Me

    Abby Jimenez

    Say You'll Remember Me is a captivating romance that masterfully balances humor and heart, following two flawed yet lovable protagonists as they navigate the complexities of love and family crisis.

    For: Fans of emotional, character-driven romantic fiction will adore this story.

    Strengths: Abby Jimenez's writing shines in crafting relatable, multi-dimensional characters and her ability to balance lighthearted humor with poignant, emotional depth.

    Skip if: While the novel's scope is ambitious, covering heavy family drama, some readers may find the pacing a bit uneven as the narrative jumps between past and present.

    Comparable: Readers who enjoy the emotional depth and humor of authors like Emily Henry, Christina Lauren, or Colleen Hoover will appreciate Say You'll Remember Me's blend of romance and realism.

    “A fun story of two people trying to give their love a (second) chance despite some huge barriers.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  12. The Night We Met (Deluxe Edition)

    Abby Jimenez

    This book stands out with its authentic portrayal of friendship, love, and the complexities of relationships, set against a relatable backdrop of everyday struggles and joys.

    For: Fans of character-driven, slow-burn romantic fiction with a touch of humor will appreciate this story.

    Strengths: Abby Jimenez masterfully explores the nuanced dynamics between friends, the bittersweet ache of unrequited love, and the resilience of the human spirit.

    Skip if: Some readers may find the pacing a bit slower than usual due to the deep character development and introspection.

    Comparable: Readers who enjoy authors like Taylor Jenkins Reid, Emily Henry, or Christina Lauren will likely resonate with Jimenez's signature blend of wit, heart, and romance.

    “Jimenez once again demonstrates a rare flair for love and laughter in this radiantly rewarding romance.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  13. The Night We Met

    Abby Jimenez

    The Night We Met is a relatable, character-driven novel with a compelling slow-burn romance, humor, and authentic portrayals of friendship and love.

    For: Readers seeking an engaging, emotional contemporary romance with well-developed characters and a thoughtful exploration of relationships.

    Strengths: Larissa and Chris's complex, witty banter shines; the supporting cast is equally well-drawn; and the exploration of friendship, loyalty, and the complexities of falling in love add depth.

    Skip if: Some readers may find the pacing a touch uneven, with occasional moments of introspection dragging the narrative.

    Comparable: Fans of Helen Hoang, Talia Hibbert, or Emma Scott will appreciate The Night We Met's character-centric approach, nuanced romance, and humor.

    “A compulsively readable slow-burn romance full of grown-up worries and plenty of pining.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  14. A Married Little Christmas (Deluxe Edition)

    Abby Jimenez

    Indulge in a delightful collection of three heartwarming holiday novellas that will leave you feeling merry and bright. Join Anna and her ex-fiancé in a hilarious fake-dating scheme, cheer on Matty as his zombie Santa stint turns into a budding romance, and laugh at Lauren's attempts to marry her whirlwind three-day boyfriend in the charming town of Little Christmas. This special deluxe edition is filled with bonus recipes, colorful illustrations, and designed edges perfect for gifting. With Abby Jimenez's signature wit and charm, these feel-good tales will have you falling in love with the season all over again.

  15. Caught Up

    Navessa Allen

    Get ready to be consumed by the dark, obsessive romance of Caught Up, the electrifying sequel to Navessa Allen's viral hit, Lights Out. When Junior, a notorious figure with a penchant for violence, becomes fixated on Lauren, a woman transformed from his high school sweetheart into a sultry social media sensation, their forbidden desires ignite a dangerous game of cat and mouse. With Junior's ruthless pursuit and Lauren's growing attachment, the line between stalker and lover blurs in this morally gray tale of second chances, kink, and the intoxicating thrill of the chase. If you're ready to be caught up in a dark romcom sensation, dive into this addictive, pulse-pounding ride.

  16. Every Version of You A Novel

    Natalie Messier

    In Natalie Messier's stirring debut, Every Version of You, Joey Vasquez returns to life at 18 - her age when she first met Elijah and Alex. Armed with the knowledge of her past, Joey sets out to win over Elijah, the love of her life. But instead, she finds herself inexplicably drawn to Alex, the man she swore she couldn't stand. As Joey navigates this second chance, she must confront the complexities of love, forgiveness, and the power of past mistakes in shaping our futures. A poignant, unforgettable romance for fans of complex characters and second chances at love.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  17. When We Cease to Understand the World

    Benjamin Labatut

    Dive into the unsettling world where scientific brilliance and madness entwine in When We Cease to Understand the World. Through vivid fiction, Benjamin Labatut reveals the complex lives of luminaries like Fritz Haber, Werner Heisenberg, and Erwin Schrödinger, whose groundbreaking discoveries led to both life-changing advancements and devastating consequences. This thought-provoking exploration plunges you into the blurred lines between genius, isolation, and the reshaping of human existence.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  18. Love Beyond Reasonable Doubt A Novel

    Swati Hegde

    In Swati Hegde's captivating romance Love Beyond Reasonable Doubt, two lawyers spontaneously ignite a passion on a Goa beach getaway, only to find themselves working together at the same firm a year later. Determined to focus solely on her career advancement, Naina must navigate the temptation of her former flame Tejas, who has never forgotten their intense summer connection. As they collaborate on a crucial case, their undeniable chemistry forces them to question everything they thought they knew about love and commitment. With its slow-burn intensity and second-chance appeal, this desi romance will enthrall fans of Nisha Sharma and Abby Jimenez.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  19. When You Loved Me A Novel

    Beatriz Williams

    Step into the enchanting world of When You Loved Me, where the lines between past and present blur. This captivating novel from Beatriz Williams follows Lucy, a young widow, as she returns to her family's estate on Winthrop Island with her daughter. Lucy's quest to uncover the truth about her estranged father's final days leads her on a thrilling journey to uncover a legendary pirate's treasure. Along the way, she must confront her own long-buried feelings, reconcile old friendships, and learn the true value of love and atonement. With its rich historical backdrop, intricate plot, and deeply emotional character arcs, When You Loved Me is a timeless tale that will transport you to the windswept shores of New England and the depths of the human heart.

Frequently asked questions

Are these books similar to 'Crazy, Stupid, Love'?
While not direct adaptations, these romances share the humor, charm, and emotional depth of that film.
Can I start with a standalone?
Yes, 'The Kiss Quotient' by Helen Hoang is an excellent standalone to begin with, featuring a plus-sized heroine with Asperger's as she seeks the perfect lover.
Are there any historical romances on this list?
No, this curated list focuses exclusively on modern, contemporary romances.
What if I prefer a slower burn romance?
If you prefer a romance to unfold at a more leisurely pace, 'Book Lovers' by Emily Henry offers a delightful, character-driven story with a realistic, slow-burning romance.
