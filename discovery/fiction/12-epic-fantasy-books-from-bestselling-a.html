12 Epic Fantasy Books from Bestselling Authors

Explore the rich worlds and unforgettable characters of these highly-acclaimed fantasy novels.

Who these books are for

  • Fantasy readers seeking epic world-building and complex characters
  • Fans of bestselling authors looking to explore new series
  • Book clubs interested in thought-provoking, high-stakes fiction

Start here

If you only read one epic fantasy series, choose Brandon Sanderson's Mistborn Saga for its inventive magic system and compelling characters.

Immerse yourself in the captivating worlds of epic fantasy with this curated collection of best-selling novels from celebrated authors. From the action-packed adventures of the Stormlight Archive to the gritty realism of the Black Company series, these books promise unforgettable journeys and the chance to explore complex, morally ambiguous characters. Whether you're a seasoned fantasy fan or looking to discover new favorite authors, this selection of epic fantasy books has something for everyone.

The collection

  1. The Empire of Gold A Novel

    S. A. Chakraborty

    Experience the thrilling conclusion to S.A. Chakraborty's acclaimed Daevabad Trilogy in The Empire of Gold. Set against a richly rendered Middle Eastern-inspired backdrop, this epic fantasy follows Nahri, Ali, and Dara as they navigate treacherous alliances, personal demons, and the fight for a magical kingdom torn apart by war. With “palace intrigue and the way that tribal infighting and war hurt the vulnerable the most” brought to vivid life, this is a must-read for fans of George R.R. Martin and epic fantasy alike.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. The Hurricane Wars A Novel

    Thea Guanzon

    In the epic fantasy romance "The Hurricane Wars", Talasyn and Prince Alaric engage in a steamy enemies-to-lovers dance amidst a nation torn apart by the deadly Hurricane Wars. With Talasyn harboring a forbidden light magic and Alaric wielding his father's ruthless shadow power, their clashing forces ignite an explosive attraction. As dark threats loom and their destinies entwine, they must decide if they can unite to save their world or risk destroying each other in the process.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. The Faith of Beasts

    James S A Corey

    Dive into a gripping, grand-scale space opera saga with The Faith of Beasts, the electrifying second volume in James S. A. Corey's critically acclaimed trilogy. As humanity faces subjugation by the monstrous Carryx empire, ruthless captive Dafyd Alkhor grapples with loyalty and purpose amidst an eternal war. Meanwhile, an agents of the empire's deathless enemy, the Swarm, infiltrates the human slaves, threatening to upend the balance of power. Expertly weaving complex characters, pulse-pounding plot twists, and vivid world-building, The Faith of Beasts is a must-read for fans of epic science fiction.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. The Spear Cuts Through Water A Novel

    Simon Jimenez

    Embark on a perilous quest with The Spear Cuts Through Water, a mesmerizing epic fantasy that blends sweeping adventure with profound explorations of identity and legacy. Two unlikely companions, haunted by past tragedy and fighting for his future, join forces with an ancient, escaped god to overthrow a tyrannical royal family and bring freedom to a broken land. This ambitious and deeply transporting novel is a must-read for anyone seeking a rich, unforgettable fantasy experience.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. The Final Strife Book One of The Ending Fire Trilogy

    Saara El-Arifi

    Immerse yourself in a world where blood determines the fate of an empire in Saara El-Arifi's electrifying debut, The Final Strife. Three women from different walks of life - Sylah, a former resistance fighter; Anoor, a disillusioned elite; and Hassa, a mysterious invisible - unite against a tyrannical red-blooded ruling class. As they navigate treacherous trials, a sizzling romance ignites, and the stage is set for a revolution that will forever reshape the kingdom. With its unique African-Arabian mythology, morally complex characters, and pulse-pounding action, this epic fantasy trilogy opener will leave you breathless and craving more.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. Fire & Blood 300 Years Before A Game of Thrones

    George R. R. Martin

    Embark on a thrilling journey through the centuries-spanning history of House Targaryen, from Aegon the Conqueror to the brutal Dance of the Dragons. In this masterful chronicle, George R.R. Martin reveals the untold tales of the dragonlords, their struggles for power, and the dark secrets behind their enigmatic reign. With vivid descriptions of legendary battles, politics, and the enigmatic magic of the First Men, Fire & Blood is a must-read for any fan of A Song of Ice and Fire series and epic fantasy alike.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. Chronicles of the Black Company

    Glen Cook

    Experience the gritty, realistic fantasy world of the Black Company through this gripping omnibus by Glen Cook, the genre's acknowledged pioneer. Chronicles of the Black Company combines the original trilogy - The Black Company, Shadows Linger, and The White Rose - to deliver a hard-hitting, morally complex saga of mercenaries in a dark, unforgiving realm. Prepare to be drawn into a world where the line between good and evil is blurred and survival is a daily battle.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. Gardens of the Moon Book One of The Malazan Book of the Fallen

    Steven Erikson

    Embark on an epic fantasy journey with Steven Erikson's Gardens of the Moon, the first installment in his majestic Malazan Book of the Fallen series. Immerse yourself in a vast world where warring armies, enigmatic gods, daring mages, and legendary creatures clash in a struggle that will determine the fate of the Malazan Empire. With intricate world-building, complex characters, and relentless action, Gardens of the Moon sets the stage for an unforgettable adventure that will keep you enthralled to the very end.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. Warbreaker A Cosmere Novel

    Brandon Sanderson

    In a world where the power of the divine hangs in the balance, two princesses, a cynical god, and an enigmatic sword weaver must navigate treachery and prophesied bloodshed to prevent war and uncover long-forgotten truths. With its unique magic system, richly detailed world-building, and morally complex characters, Brandon Sanderson's standalone epic Warbreaker is a captivating journey that will transport and enthrall readers.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. The Way of Kings Book One of the Stormlight Archive

    Brandon Sanderson

    Epic fantasy at its most ambitious, with a richly detailed world, intricate magic system, and complex characters, The Way of Kings sets the stage for a sprawling, epic saga.

    For: Fans of high fantasy, particularly those drawn to detailed world-building, complex magic, and character-driven stories.

    Strengths: Sanderson's unique magic system and world-building are unparalleled in their depth and creativity, while the characters are multi-dimensional and compelling.

    Skip if: Given the scope of the world and story, this is a dense, slow-burning novel that may not appeal to readers seeking a fast-paced, action-oriented tale.

    Comparable: For readers who enjoy massive, detailed fantasy series like Robert Jordan's Wheel of Time, George R.R. Martin's A Song of Ice and Fire, or Patrick Rothfuss' Kingkiller Chronicle, The Way of Kings offers a similarly epic and immersive experience.

    “Sanderson raises the genre stakes… A fan favorite.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  11. Words of Radiance Book Two of the Stormlight Archive

    Brandon Sanderson

    Words of Radiance is a richly detailed, epic fantasy that expands its immersive world, deepens complex characters, and raises the stakes in a captivating story arc.

    For: Fans of high fantasy, particularly those invested in the Stormlight Archive series, will devour this installment, while newcomers can appreciate the standalone world-building and character introductions.

    Strengths: Sanderson masterfully weaves together multiple storylines, action, and philosophical themes, while his imaginative magic systems and immersive world-building set a new standard for epic fantasy.

    Skip if: The sprawling scope and intricate plotting may overwhelm some readers, requiring patience and dedication to fully engage with the narrative.

    Comparable: For fans of epic fantasy with complex world-building, intricate magic, and large casts of characters, Words of Radiance shares similarities with other acclaimed series like The Wheel of Time and Malazan Book of the Fallen.

    “Sanderson raises the genre stakes… A fan favorite.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  12. Oathbringer Book Three of the Stormlight Archive

    Brandon Sanderson

    Oathbringer is a captivating, deeply immersive fantasy epic that expands the scope and complexity of the world and characters, with a richly detailed story that rewards careful attention.

    For: Fantasy fans seeking a richly detailed, epic story with complex characters and world-building.

    Strengths: Masterful world-building, intricate plotting, well-developed characters, and a sweeping narrative that explores themes of power, morality, and unity.

    Skip if: Skip if you prefer fast-paced action over detailed descriptions and philosophical musings.

    Comparable: Fans of epic fantasy series like Patrick Rothfuss' Kingkiller Chronicle or George R.R. Martin's A Song of Ice and Fire will appreciate the depth and scale of the Stormlight Archive.

    “Sanderson raises the genre stakes… A fan favorite.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  13. Rhythm of War Book Four of the Stormlight Archive

    Brandon Sanderson

    Rhythm of War is a highly anticipated sequel in Brandon Sanderson's epic Stormlight Archive series, continuing the intense world-building, complex characters, and thrilling plot that fans of epic fantasy have come to expect from the author.

    For: Fans of epic fantasy, particularly those invested in the Stormlight Archive series, will greatly enjoy this installment.

    Strengths: Sanderson's masterful world-building, intricate magical systems, and well-developed characters drive the story forward at a breakneck pace, making this a gripping read for fantasy enthusiasts.

    Skip if: New readers may find it challenging to fully engage with the complex world and characters without prior knowledge of the series, so it's best suited for those who have already read the preceding books.

    Comparable: Rhythm of War shares similarities with other epic fantasy series like George R.R. Martin's A Song of Ice and Fire and Patrick Rothfuss's Kingkiller Chronicle in terms of its sprawling world, morally complex characters, and intricate plotting.

    “Sanderson raises the genre stakes… A fan favorite.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  14. A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms

    George R. R. Martin

    Step into the Seven Kingdoms nearly a century before the events of A Game of Thrones in this riveting prequel trio from George R.R. Martin. Meet Ser Duncan the Tall, a towering knight with a heart of gold, and his loyal diminutive squire Egg, as they navigate a world of royal intrigue, powerful foes, and outrageous exploits. Featuring over 160 captivating illustrations, A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms is a must-read that seamlessly weaves rich history and vivid characters, making it a thrilling companion to Martin's iconic A Song of Ice and Fire series.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  15. Empire of the Vampire Book One

    Jay Kristoff

    "Embark on a journey into the heart of eternal darkness with Jay Kristoff's Empire of the Vampire. This gripping dark fantasy saga opens with Gabriel de León, the last silversaint, imprisoned by the vampires he swore to defeat. Relive epic battles, forbidden love, and the quest for humanity's last hope in this richly imagined world, illuminated by haunting illustrations. A must-read for fans of immersive, action-packed fantasy."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  16. Mistborn The Final Empire

    Brandon Sanderson

    Mistborn is a landmark epic fantasy that masterfully blends gripping heist elements, political intrigue, and world-shaping magic, elevating the genre with its unique magic system and intricate plotting.

    For: Fantasy fans seeking a rich, immersive, and thrilling series with complex characters and a sprawling world.

    Strengths: Mistborn excels at crafting a captivating world, developing a compelling cast, and delivering unexpected twists and turns, all while exploring thought-provoking themes.

    Skip if: The dense world-building and intricate plotting may overwhelm some readers, requiring a significant investment of time and focus.

    Comparable: Fans of epic fantasy series like Wheel of Time, The First Law trilogy, and The Kingkiller Chronicle will find parallels in Mistborn's scope, complexity, and epic storytelling.

    “Sanderson raises the genre stakes… A fan favorite.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  17. The Well of Ascension Book Two of Mistborn

    Brandon Sanderson

    Descend into the world of Scadrial, where an assassin's deadly aim and a rebel's righteous cause collide in an epic struggle for freedom and power. In the aftermath of Heroes of Adventura, Vin and her companions must confront the dark forces awakened when the Well of Ascension is breached, threatening to plunge the planet into eternal night. With its intricate magic system, morally complex characters, and relentless pace, The Well of Ascension plunges readers back into the heart of Brandon Sanderson's Mistborn saga, raising the stakes and amplifying the excitement as Vin faces the ultimate test of her courage and conviction.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  18. The Hero of Ages Book Three of Mistborn

    Brandon Sanderson

    In the riveting conclusion to the original Mistborn trilogy, Brandon Sanderson weaves an intricate, emotionally charged tale of redemption and revolution that will captivate readers deeply invested in the epic world he's meticulously crafted. "The Hero of Ages" masterfully balances pulse-pounding adventure, complex political intrigue, and profound character arcs, culminating in a wildly satisfying, memory-searing finish that will leave you breathless and forever changed by the Sanderson experience.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  19. Wind and Truth Book Five of the Stormlight Archive

    Brandon Sanderson

    In the heart-pounding conclusion to the first arc of the epic Stormlight Archive series, Brandon Sanderson masterfully weaves together a multitude of threads as the fate of Roshar hangs in the balance. With the world on the brink of destruction, beloved characters like Kaladin, Shallan, and Dalinar engage in desperate battles and alliances that will reshape the very fabric of the Cosmere. Prepare to be swept away in a whirlwind of action, magic, and emotional depth as the stage is set for the next epic chapter in this sprawling fantasy masterpiece.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  20. Chronicles of the Black Company

    Glen Cook

    "From the mastermind of grimdark fantasy, Glen Cook, comes the epic Chronicles of the Black Company - a gripping trilogy that will draw you into a world of hard-bitten mercenaries, where morality is a luxury few can afford. Join the soldiers of the Black Company as they navigate a treacherous landscape of war, politics, and prophecy, all set against a backdrop of dark magic and bitter steel. With its gritty realism, complex characters, and unflinching portrayal of the dark side of humanity, this omnibus marks a foundational work in the grimdark genre - a must-read for any fan of modern fantasy."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  21. The Priory of the Orange Tree

    Samantha Shannon

    Step into a breathtaking realm where powerful women forge their destinies, dragons soar, and ancient secrets threaten to unravel the future. In this immersive high fantasy world, Queen Sabran must navigate treacherous alliances and hidden threats to ensure the survival of her queendom, while Ead, a loyal lady-in-waiting, fights to protect the throne with her forbidden magic. With a sprawling cast of characters, epic battles, and tantalizing mysteries, The Priory of the Orange Tree is a rich, addictive tale that will instantly draw you into its enchanting world.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  22. Songs of the Dead

    Brandon Sanderson

    Step into the layered world of London, where the past and present collide in a battle for history itself. In Brandon Sanderson and Peter Orullian's Songs of the Dead, Jack Solomon, a young musician, finds himself caught between life and death when he's killed - only to uncover a magical society wielding the power of light and music. As Jack navigates the spiraling strata of London's hidden eras, from ancient Roman times to the glamour of the West End, he must confront a rising tide of anger among the dead, with the fate of all eras hanging in the balance. With Sanderson's masterful worldbuilding and Orullian's keen music knowledge, Songs of the Dead delivers a thrilling, contemporary fantasy that will keep you enthralled until the very end.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

Frequently asked questions

What's the best epic fantasy book to start with?
Brandon Sanderson's 'The Way of Kings', the first book in the Stormlight Archive, is an excellent starting point due to its well-developed world and relatable characters.
Are these books part of a series?
Yes, most books in this collection are part of larger series, allowing readers to dive into rich, expansive worlds with complex story arcs.
Are the authors primarily known for epic fantasy?
While some authors, like Brandon Sanderson, are renowned for their epic fantasy works, others like George R.R. Martin have also made significant contributions to the genre
