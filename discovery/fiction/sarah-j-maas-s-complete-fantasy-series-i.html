Sarah J. Maas's Complete Fantasy Series in Order

Dive into the epic worlds of Celaena, Feyre, and more with this comprehensive list of Sarah J. Maas's fantasy series in the correct reading order.

Who these books are for

  • Fans of Sarah J. Maas and her fantasy series
  • Readers looking to explore new epic fantasy worlds
  • Book lovers seeking a complete series reading list

Start here

If you only read one fantasy series by Sarah J. Maas, choose Throne of Glass, her first and most popular series.

Sarah J. Maas has captivated readers worldwide with her sprawling, intricate, and romantic fantasy epics. Her works span multiple series, each with its own unique magic system, characters, and worldbuilding. This guide provides an authoritative list of her novels and novellas in the correct reading order, helping fans and new readers alike navigate her extensive bibliography and immerse themselves in the rich fantasy realms she creates.

The collection

  1. A Court of Thorns and Roses

    Sarah J. Maas

    Step into Faerie and be entranced by this lush, action-packed retelling of Beauty and the Beast. Sarah J. Maas masterfully weaves together a captivating tale of forbidden love, dark secrets, and fierce heroism as mortal Aelin navigates the treacherous world of the Spring Court faeries. With its spellbinding magic, unforgettable characters, and pulse-pounding plot, A Court of Thorns and Roses is an enchanting read that will leave you breathless for more.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. A Court of Mist and Fury

    Sarah J. Maas

    Embrace the captivating world of Prythian as Feyre, the captive princess, awakens to the seductive allure of the Night Court. In this darkly enchanting follow-up to 'A Court of Thorns and Roses,' Sarah J. Maas masterfully weaves a tale of power, desire, and the unbreakable bonds of love. As Feyre navigates the treacherous politics of the fey realms and confronts the shadows within, she must choose between the man she once loved and the one who sets her soul on fire, forging a new destiny that will shake the very foundations of her world.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. A Court of Wings and Ruin

    Sarah J. Maas

    Diving into 'A Court of Wings and Ruin' is an immersive experience that will have you breathless and emotionally invested from the start. Sarah J. Maas masterfully weaves a lush, enchanting world with complex characters whose triumphs and heartaches resonate deeply. The romance is slow-burn perfection, the action is pulse-pounding, and the revelations will leave you reeling. Be prepared to lose yourself completely in this captivating tale of love, loyalty, and the unbreakable bonds of family.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. A Court of Frost and Starlight

    Sarah J. Maas

    Join Feyre, Rhysand, and their friends as they celebrate a hard-won Winter Solstice in A Court of Frost and Starlight. This tender, character-driven tale bridges the events of the previous books, delving into the lasting impacts of war and the unbreakable bonds of fierce love within the Night Court. A must-read for fans of the series, this poignant addition offers a rare glimpse into the characters' emotional healing and the joyful, hopeful moments that follow the darkest struggles.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. A Court of Silver Flames

    Sarah J. Maas

    In A Court of Silver Flames, New York Times bestselling author Sarah J. Maas masterfully weaves a captivating tale of forbidden desire and scorched hearts as Nesta Archeron, Feyre's spirited sister, finds herself ensnared in a tempestuous romance with the battle-hardened warrior Cassian. Against the backdrop of a world grappling with the aftermath of war, Nesta and Cassian must confront their dark pasts and the fiery passion that threatens to consume them. Maas's vivid world-building and scorching character development make this a must-read for fans of the series and fantasy romance alike.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. Throne of Glass

    Sarah J. Maas

    Throne of Glass launches a captivating, high-stakes fantasy series with a strong, complex heroine and intricate world-building.

    For: Fans of epic fantasy, especially those who enjoy strong female protagonists, will devour this series.

    Strengths: Celaena's character development, the intricate plot with its twists and turns, and the lush, immersive world all shine in this debut.

    Skip if: Some readers may find the initial pacing slow as the story sets up the world and characters.

    Comparable: For similar immersive, character-driven fantasy, check out the Court of Thorns and Roses series by the same author.

    “An epic fantasy readers will immerse themselves in and never want to leave.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. Crown of Midnight

    Sarah J. Maas

    In Crown of Midnight, the thrilling sequel to Throne of Glass, Sarah J. Maas masterfully weaves a tale of deception, danger, and dark magic set in a treacherously divided kingdom. As the King's Champion, Celaena must navigate treacherous court politics while secretly seeking justice, her loyalties torn between rival factions. With the line between friend and foe blurred and a terrible tragedy looming, Celaena must confront the darkness within herself and decide what she's truly fight for in this epic, action-packed fantasy romance.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. Heir of Fire

    Sarah J. Maas

    Heir of Fire is a thrilling, action-packed addition to the Throne of Glass series, offering epic fantasy, complex worldbuilding, and a strong, dynamic heroine.

    For: Fans of young adult fantasy, particularly those who enjoy strong female protagonists and intricate, sprawling worldscapes.

    Strengths: Maas masterfully weaves together multiple storylines, deepens character development, and raises the stakes in this pivotal installment.

    Skip if: At over 600 pages, this is a substantial, dense read that requires a significant time commitment.

    Comparable: Readers who appreciate the Court of Thorns and Roses series by the same author will find similar epic fantasy elements and romantic intrigue in Heir of Fire.

    “An epic fantasy readers will immerse themselves in and never want to leave.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. Queen of Shadows

    Sarah J. Maas

    In Queen of Shadows, Sarah J. Maas propels Aelin Galathynius, once a skilled assassin, now the rightful queen of Terrasen, into an explosive battle for power and revenge. With her loved ones captured and her realm enslaved, Aelin forges an unbreakable resolve to fight for all she holds dear. The thrilling fourth installment of the Throne of Glass series promises pulse-pounding action, sizzling romance, and the unyielding spirit of a queen determined to reclaim her throne and exact her vengeance.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. Empire of Storms

    Sarah J. Maas

    Empire of Storms brings a thrilling conclusion to the Throne of Glass series, packed with action, magic, and emotional depth.

    For: Fans of epic fantasy, especially those who follow the Throne of Glass series, will love this installment.

    Strengths: This book expertly balances plot and character development, delivering a satisfying finale that ties up loose ends while intensifying the stakes and relationships.

    Skip if: New readers may find the extensive backstory and character arcs overwhelming, so it's best for those already invested in the series.

    Comparable: Similar in tone and scope to other fantasy series like A Song of Ice and Fire or The Wheel of Time, Empire of Storms is a must-read for fans of sprawling, immersive fantasy worlds.

    “An epic fantasy readers will immerse themselves in and never want to leave.”

  11. Tower of Dawn

    Sarah J. Maas

    Dear friend, if you're craving an epic fantasy with swoon-worthy romance and pulse-pounding battles, look no further than Sarah J. Maas's 'Tower of Dawn'! This captivating installment in the Throne of Glass series shifts the narrative spotlight to Chaol Westfall, a brooding and complex character, as he navigates the treacherous world of Alderaan. With Maas's signature magic and heart-stirring emotions, 'Tower of Dawn' is perfect for fans of morally gray heroes, intricate world-building, and the thrill of adventure. Get ready to lose yourself in this unforgettable tale!

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  12. Kingdom of Ash

    Sarah J. Maas

    Kingdom of Ash is a satisfying conclusion to the Throne of Glass series, delivering epic fantasy with complex characters, rich world-building, and a gripping narrative.

    For: Fans of epic fantasy, those invested in the Throne of Glass series, and readers seeking a heroic, action-packed story with a strong female protagonist.

    Strengths: Maas masterfully weaves together multiple storylines, crafting a thrilling, emotionally resonant tale with well-developed characters and a fully realized fantasy world.

    Skip if: At nearly 800 pages, this is a time commitment; some readers may find the dense, detailed world-building and multiple POVs a challenge to follow at times.

    Comparable: For readers of The Throne of Glass series, other epic fantasy series like The Wheel of Time or A Song of Ice and Fire will offer similar scope and complexity.

    “An epic fantasy readers will immerse themselves in and never want to leave.”

  13. A Court of Thorns and Roses 6

    Sarah J. Maas

    The sixth installment in the beloved Court of Thorns and Roses series, it delivers on the fantasy, romance, and epic storytelling fans adore.

    For: Longtime fans of the series and readers of adult fantasy romance will eagerly devour this new chapter.

    Strengths: Maas masterfully weaves intricate world-building, complex characters, and intense emotional arcs, with a slow-burn romance and high stakes.

    Skip if: With a sprawling cast and detailed lore, new readers may struggle to fully engage without prior knowledge of the series.

    Comparable: Readers of adult fantasy series like Throne of Glass and Crescent City will find a similarly immersive, passionate, and magical experience.

    Step back into the captivating world of Prythian with bestselling author Sarah J. Maas in this eagerly anticipated seventh installment of the Court of Thorns and Roses series. Picking up where the previous tale left off, Maas masterfully weaves her signature blend of romance, magic, and high-stakes intrigue, ensuring another unforgettable journey for fans of this beloved fantasy saga.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best starting point for a new Sarah J. Maas reader?
Begin with Throne of Glass, the first novel in the original Celaena Sardothien series.
Are the A Court of Thorns and Roses series and Throne of Glass connected?
While set in different worlds, both series share elements of fantasy, romance, and strong female protagonists, appealing to similar readers.
What format should I read Sarah J. Maas's books in?
All novels are available in hardcover, paperback, e-book, and audiobook formats from major retailers.
