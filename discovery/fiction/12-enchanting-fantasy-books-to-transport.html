12 Enchanting Fantasy Books to Transport You to Magical Realms

Escape into bewitching worlds of fairies, witches, and mythical creatures with these spellbinding fantasy novels.

Who these books are for

  • Fantasy readers seeking immersive, magical worlds
  • Fans of witches, faeries, and mythical creatures
  • Adults and young adults looking for captivating stories
  • Book clubs interested in diverse fantasy themes

Start here

If you only read one book about magical realism, choose 'The Night Circus' by Erin Morgenstern for its mesmerizing atmosphere and unique premise.

Step into the enchanting realms of these 12 fantasy novels, where magic is woven into the fabric of everyday life. From witches and faeries to demons and mythical creatures, immerse yourself in captivating stories that transport you to new and imaginative worlds. Whether you're seeking urban fantasy, historical fantasy, or epic tales of good vs. evil, this collection offers a diverse array of magical experiences to fuel your imagination and satisfy your craving for the extraordinary.

The collection

  1. This Will Be Interesting A Novel

    E. B. Asher

    Join Galwell True, resurrected legendary hero, River Pricemark, deadly assassin, Queen Thessia, ready to defy damsel tropes, and Mona Grandhart, charming Vestriyan outlaw, on a thrilling, hilarious quest. With danger lurking at every turn, from underground lairs to magical masquerades, they must navigate escalating impersonations, friendship's inimitable power, and the most notorious horseball tournament in the realm - all while strictly adhering to the one rule no one will follow: no romance. Prepare for a slow burn, cozy, and laugh-out-loud fantastical adventure featuring sapphic friends-to-lovers, reluctant allies-to-lovers, and reluctant fake marriage, as they discover even the most improbable of heroes and heroines can make the most

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. Mestra A Short Story

    Madeline Miller

    A captivating tale of love, sacrifice, and the blurred lines between help and harm, Mestra is a masterful reimagining of mythology by #1 NYT bestselling author Madeline Miller. When Poseidon gifts Mestra, daughter of a cursed king, the power to transform into any being, she must grapple with whether to use her gift to sustain her father's insatiable hunger or forge her own path to freedom. A jewel-like exploration of human fallibility, Mestra confirms Miller as our premier mythologist, weaving an unforgettable story of a daughter's devotion pushed to its limits.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. Best Served Cold

    Joe Abercrombie

    In the brutal world of Best Served Cold, vengeance and power collide as mercenary Monza Murcatto seeks bloody retribution against her betrayers, all while evading the deadly forces arrayed against her. With a motley crew of misfit allies and a ruthless pursuer hot on her heels, this gripping fantasy offers a dark, visceral thrill ride that will leave you breathless.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. That Time I Got Drunk and Saved a Demon

    Kimberly Lemming

    "Join Cinnamon on an unforgettable adventure as this quirky spice trader turns her quiet life upside down to save a fiery demon from certain doom. With a dash of humor, steamy tension, and heart, this irreverent rom-com fantasy is the perfect escape from reality. Let the unpredictable road trip with Fallon begin and discover why readers are raving about this fresh voice in fantasy romance."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. One Dark Window (Standard Hardcover Edition)

    Rachel Gillig

    This dark, lush fantasy enthralls with its unique magic system, intricate world-building, and slow-burn romance, making it a captivating read.

    For: Fans of dark fantasy, gothic settings, and slow-burn romances will find plenty to love in this tale.

    Strengths: One Dark Window shines in its vivid, atmospheric prose and complex characters, particularly its strong female lead and a brooding, mysterious love interest.

    Skip if: Some readers may find the pacing a bit slow, as the story builds an immersive world and characters before escalating into action.

    Comparable: Comparable to works like Uprooted, For the Wolf, and dark fairy tales by Robin McKinley, One Dark Window weaves together elements of fantasy, romance, and mystery into an enchanting whole.

    “A tightly written duology that manages to build a dark, twisty world, unique magic system and well-written characters ... rich and fast-paced.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. Twelve Months

    Jim Butcher

    In the aftermath of a devastating battle, wizard Harry Dresden struggles to rebuild his city and himself. As ghouls terrorize Chicago, a dying brother and a Faerie queen's deadly alliance await, forcing Harry to confront his own emotional scars and rising darkness. Can this hard-hit hero reclaim his power and purpose to save his beloved home once more? Dive into the twelfth book of Jim Butcher's epic Dresden Files series for a gripping, emotionally charged thrill ride.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. The Very Secret Society of Irregular Witches A romantic cozy fantasy

    Sangu Mandanna

    This enchanting novel will transport readers to a whimsical world of magic, found family, and romance, perfect for fans of cozy fantasy and heartwarming stories.

    For: Readers seeking a charming, slow-burn romance with a touch of magic and a strong sense of belonging will adore this book.

    Strengths: The Very Secret Society of Irregular Witches shines in its vivid world-building, lovable characters, and the author's skillful balance of humor, warmth, and emotional depth.

    Skip if: Some readers may find the pacing a tad slow, as the story unfolds at a leisurely, character-driven pace.

    Comparable: For those who enjoy magical realism, found family stories, and gentle romances, this novel is akin to works by authors like Sarah Addison Allen, Erin Morgenstern, or Deborah Harkness.

    “This gorgeously cozy romantic fantasy sparkles with real magic, love, and joy. A perfect comfort read.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. A Witch's Guide to Magical Innkeeping

    Sangu Mandanna

    A Witch's Guide to Magical Innkeeping is a heartwarming, whimsical tale of a witch's quest to reclaim her powers and find belonging, weaving together elements of magic, humor, and found family.

    For: Readers seeking a cozy, character-driven fantasy with a strong focus on relationships and personal growth.

    Strengths: Mandanna's vivid world-building and endearing characters make this novel a delightful read, while its themes of identity, purpose, and the power of connection resonate deeply.

    Skip if: Some may find the pacing a tad slow as the story unfolds at a leisurely, inn-like pace.

    Comparable: Fans of whimsical fantasy with strong magical realism elements, such as The Night Circus by Erin Morgenstern or The All-Consuming World by Constance Sayers, will likely appreciate this charming tale.

    “I want to live inside this wonderful book! Every single page is sweet, quirky, charming, and one hundred percent delightful! I absolutely loved it!”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. Emily Wilde's Encyclopaedia of Faeries

    Heather Fawcett

    Prepare for an enchanting journey with Cambridge professor Emily Wilde, a brilliant but prickly scholar, as she embarks on a quest to uncover the secrets of faeries in the remote village of Hrafnsvik. Battling her own Social Skills Deficiency, Emily must navigate awkward encounters with lovable locals and her dashing but infuriating rival Wendell Bambleby. As she delves deeper into the mysteries of the Hidden Ones, Emily uncovers a web of wonder, magic, and unexpected romance in this delightful, heartwarming, and darkly gorgeous fantasy story.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. Witches of Dubious Origin

    Jenn McKinlay

    Step into a magical world of mystery and romance with Jenn McKinlay's Witches of Dubious Origin. When librarian Zoe Ziakas uncovers she's a witch, she must harness her ancestral powers and decode a mysterious grimoire before dark forces catch on. Joined by the handsome Jasper Griffin, Zoe's enchanting journey to confront her legacy weaves together history, humor, and heart, making this a spellbinding read you won't want to put down.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  11. Thistlemarsh

    Moorea Corrigan

    In the aftermath of World War I, orphaned Mouse Dunne inherits the enigmatic Thistlemarsh Hall, a crumbling English manor with a Faerie blessing. Desperate to save her home and care for her shell-shocked brother, Mouse makes a deal with a handsome, arrogant Faerie to restore the manor within a month. As dark secrets and magical forces threaten her very existence, Mouse must confront the ghosts of her past and the mysteries of her heart in this captivating tale of love, revenge, and redemption perfect for fans of Robin McKinley, Mercedes Lackey, and A Court of Thorns and Roses.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  12. The Book Witch A Novel

    Meg Shaffer

    This enchanting tale celebrates the magic of reading and the power of stories, inviting readers to step into the world of fiction alongside its endearing protagonist.

    For: Book lovers of all ages seeking a whimsical, imaginative adventure will delight in this charming novel.

    Strengths: The Book Witch shines in its creative premise, blending fantasy and literature with heartfelt explorations of family, friendship, and the transformative nature of stories.

    Skip if: Some readers may find the pacing a bit slow as the narrative weaves through various literary landscapes.

    Comparable: Fans of Harry Potter's magical universe and the literary escapades in Jasper Fforde's Thursday Next series will appreciate this unique take on the intersection of fiction and reality.

    “Meg Shaffer continues to surprise and delight me with each book she writes.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  13. The Fragile Threads of Power

    V. E. Schwab

    V. E. Schwab masterfully weaves together three interconnected worlds, each with its own unique magic system and complex characters, crafting a rich, immersive fantasy that will captivate readers.

    For: Fans of epic fantasy, complex world-building, and morally ambiguous characters will find this a compelling addition to their reading list.

    Strengths: The Fragile Threads of Power excels at building intricate worlds, developing complex characters, and crafting a suspenseful plot that spans multiple storylines.

    Skip if: At over 500 pages, this is a dense, detailed read requiring a significant time commitment.

    Comparable: Readers who enjoy the complex world-building and morally gray characters of Patrick Rothfuss's Kingkiller Chronicle series will likely appreciate this work's similar depth and complexity.

    “Schwab cleverly builds on her existing worlds, introducing new threats and expanding the magic system. The new characters captivate and the plot twists shock.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  14. The Magpie Lord A Charm of Magpies, #1

    KJ Charles

    In this enchanting historical romance, Lucien Vaudrey, Lord Crane, returns to England with secrets and enemies, only to find his crumbling estate beset by dark forces. Desperate for help, he turns to Stephen Day, a prickly yet captivating magician haunted by Crane's family legacy. As drawn to each other as they are to the supernatural threats surrounding them, they must navigate treacherous magic, hidden agendas, and the ever-present peril of death - all while forging an unlikely alliance that might just save them both from the shadows.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  15. The Bear and the Nightingale A Novel

    Katherine Arden

    Immerse yourself in this enchanting tale of magic, mystery, and the darker side of folklore, where a spirited young girl named Vasilisa must navigate the treacherous consequences of ignoring ancient traditions and household spirits in the icy grip of 9th-century Russia. With lush, lyrical prose reminiscent of timeless fairy tales, The Bear and the Nightingale is a captivating first installment in the Winternight Trilogy that will transport you to a world of myth and wonder.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  16. Fate's Bane

    C. L. Clark

    In the precarious peace of the fens, fate intertwines the lives of Agnir and the chieftain's daughter in a tragic sapphic adventure. As they navigate warring clans and hidden magic, their forbidden love kindles a power that could unite their families at last, or shatter the fragile truce forever. Dive into C. L. Clark's stirring and sensual tale of loyalty, ambition, and the legend that exacts its price.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  17. Brigands & Breadknives

    Travis Baldree

    Join Fern, the eccentric and irreverent bookseller from the hit Legends & Lattes series, as she navigates a fresh and unexpected adventure in Brigands & Breadknives. Leaving behind the comfort of familiar surroundings, Fern finds herself entangled with a misfit crew of a legendary warrior, a silverware-loving goblin, and a heartbreaking hangover. As they battle a motley of rogues after the goblin's bounty, Fern must confront her own ennui and discover the courage to chart a bold new path in this thrilling and humorous fantasy escapade. A must-read for fans of fantasy and those seeking a compelling tale of self-discovery and unlikely friendship.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  18. Red Rabbit

    Alex Grecian

    Ride with a ragtag crew of outlaws, hunters, and misfits on a perilous, supernatural journey through the Wild West in Alex Grecian's pulse-pounding novel, Red Rabbit. As they pursue a wanted witch across a demon-infested landscape, the line between life and death blurs at every dusty turn. With its breakneck pace, eerie atmosphere, and unforgettable characters, this dark fantasy will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very end. Strap in for the wildest ride of the year.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  19. A Darker Shade of Magic A Novel

    V. E. Schwab

    Step into a thrilling world where magic thrives in hidden parallel Londons. Meet Kell, an Antari smuggler, and Delilah, a feisty thief, as they embark on a perilous quest to save their worlds from treachery and danger. With vivid characters, immersive magic, and a pulse-pounding plot, V.E. Schwab's A Darker Shade of Magic will keep you captivated until the very end.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  20. Field Guide for the Formerly Villainous (Deluxe Edition)

    Autumn K England

    Escape to Mossley's Rest, where a wounded former villain finds solace in simple living, budding friendships, and a forbidden romance. Autumn England's whimsical fantasy, starring a relatable and endearing protagonist Oaklin, crafts a heartwarming tale of healing and redemption amidst the enchanting backdrop of a magical farm. Rich with humor, charm, and a touch of darkness, this delightful read, reminiscent of Stardew Valley and Studio Ghibli, is perfect for cozy fantasy fans seeking a feel-good story.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  21. Practical Magic

    Alice Hoffman

    Discover the enchanting tale of Sally and Gillian Owens, sisters struggling to reconcile their magical heritage with ordinary lives in this bewitching novel. As they navigate a world where powers lurk in shadows, they must confront their eerie aunts, a menacing backyard ghost, and ultimately, embrace their gifts for a future of love and passion. Alice Hoffman weaves a hauntingly funny and shamelessly romantic narrative that will leave you spellbound.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  22. The Impossible Garden of Clara Thorne A Cozy, Sapphic Romantasy with Gardens, a Quest, and Lots of Spice!

    Summer N England

    Immerse yourself in a charming tale of magic, love, and self-discovery as Clara Thorne, a talented but troubled gardener, embarks on a quest to bring life to a cursed town. Alongside her charming, if infuriating, protector Hesper, Clara must confront her deepest fears and unlock her hidden potential. With its lighthearted humor, steamy romance, and whimsical fantasy elements, The Impossible Garden of Clara Thorne is a delightful, feel-good read perfect for fans of cozy romantasy seeking a captivating, heartwarming journey.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  23. Eminence Front

    Rebecca Rowland

    In Rebecca Rowland's chilling winter horror novel, Eminence Front, a snowstorm unleashes an ancient curse on a small New England town, targeting a single street with unearthly whispers and malevolent entities that delve into residents' darkest secrets and regrets. As the storm intensifies, each homeowner is drawn into a waking nightmare, forced to confront the cosmic evil head-on. With its eerie atmosphere and gripping plot, this Shirley Jackson and Bram Stoker Award finalist is a must-read for horror fans and anyone seeking a literary thrill ride.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  24. Twig's Traveling Tomes

    Gryffin Murphy

    "Embark on a mesmerizing journey through time and imagination with Twig's Traveling Tomes, Gryffin Murphy's enchanting literary odyssey. As our protagonist curates a whimsical collection of rare books, each volume transports us to a captivating realm - a mysterious forest, an opulent palace, and even a floating city. With lyrical prose and imaginative storytelling, Murphy weaves a tapestry of wonder that explores the power of knowledge, the allure of the unknown, and the magic found within the pages of a treasured tale. Get lost in the spell of Twig's Tomes, where the boundaries of reality blur and the possibilities are endless."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  25. Obscura A Novel

    Raelle Tucker

    Step into the mesmerizing world of Obscura, a hidden Mediterranean island where mythic beings have fled centuries of persecution to build a secret utopia. But when Artemis, a girl with her own secrets, spares a trespassing tourist and smuggles him into her community, a seismic reckoning begins. As lies, hypocrisies, and brutality are exposed, Artemis must decide whether to protect her world or shatter it to reveal the truth, setting a thrilling tale ablaze with action, romance, and a love letter to outsiders. With its genre-bending blend of fantasy, literary fiction, and gritty realism, Obscura will captivate fans of dark, intoxicating tales that push the boundaries of convention.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  26. The Reimagining of Thornwood House

    Jaleigh Johnson

    Join land witch Evelyn Sharpe and her ward Ruby on a magical journey as they discover the enchanted Thornwood House. This sentient home, the heart of the village of Iskendra, has a mind of its own and is not eager to welcome Evie and Ruby. As the two witches work to form bonds with Thornwood and its quirky inhabitants, they uncover a deep-rooted darkness threatening the town's magic. With gentle hearts and wild powers, they must unravel the secrets of the mysterious house to protect their newfound home and community in this cozy, enchanting tale by Jaleigh Johnson.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  27. This Damsel Is Not In Distress A Cozy Fantasy Romance

    Tee Harlowe

    Enter a whimsical world of magic and romance with "This Damsel Is Not In Distress". When Niamh Merridon flees her war-torn kingdom, she finds refuge in a sentient castle. But her peace is short-lived, as a prophecy links her to a prince and a gruff bodyguard, Rafe Wolfgang, who challenges her wit and ignites forbidden feelings. As Niamh navigates the castle's shifting rooms and secrets, she must resist Rafe's tempting charm and unravel the mystery before her life and heart are lost. A delightful blend of cozy fantasy, spicy romance, and mischievous magic awaits in this standalone tale.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

Frequently asked questions

Are these books part of a series or standalone?
Most are standalone novels, with a few starting series. Check individual book descriptions for details.
What fantasy sub-genres are represented?
This collection spans various fantasy realms, including magical realism, urban fantasy, historical fantasy, and epic fantasy.
Are there any books with strong LGBTQ+ themes?
Yes, several titles feature diverse representation, including same-sex relationships, non-binary characters, and exploration of gender identity.
Can I find books with a strong focus on magic and spells?
Absolutely! Many books center around witchcraft, magical abilities, and the intricacies of spell-casting in their respective worlds.
