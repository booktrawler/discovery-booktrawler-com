12 Books About Dark Fantasy Villains

Dive into the dark side with these 12 gripping novels featuring complex villains and morally ambiguous worlds.

Who these books are for

  • Fans of morally complex characters and dark fantasy settings
  • Readers seeking a fresh take on the traditional hero-villain dynamic
  • Anyone enjoying fantasy, horror, or speculative fiction

Start here

If you only read one book about morally ambiguous villains, choose 'Blood & Betrayals' by Jeanette Rose for a gripping tale of loyalty, deceit, and power.

Venture into the darker corners of fantasy with this curated selection of 12 novels that put complex, intriguing villains at the forefront. From gritty urban fantasies to epic high fantasies, these stories explore the gray areas between good and evil, often blurring the lines altogether. Prepare to be captivated by the moral ambiguities and gripping characters that drive these unforgettable tales.

The collection

  1. Villain A Novel

    Natalie Zina Walschots

    In the electrifying sequel to Hench, Natalie Zina Walschots thrusts readers back into the wicked world of diabolical henchpeople. The Auditor, a brilliant and queer protagonist, has her sights set on The Draft, the sinister organization that nurtures the world's most destructive superheroes. As she navigates a treacherous landscape filled with former foes, unexpected allies, and the inconstant affections of her enigmatic boss, the Auditor must confront a new and terrifying opponent - The Draft's cunning Chief Marketing Officer. This razor-sharp, hilarious, and violent ride is a must for fans of dark humor and subversive takes on the supervillain genre.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. Grim Tidings A Novel

    B.K. Borison

    Meet Darcy, a rebellious Grim Reaper determined to defy her destiny and save a soul instead of claiming it. But when her plan clashes with a skeptical Guardian Angel, Gabriel Aetos, an unlikely partnership beyond the grave ensues. As they navigate a tangled web of magic and corporate hauntings, their growing feelings for each other threaten to upend everything. Grim Tidings is a spooky yet simmering enemies-to-lovers tale of star-crossed lovers that will keep you captivated until the very end.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. Six Wild Crowns

    Holly Race

    Immerse yourself in a dazzling fantasy reimagining of Henry VIII's court, where politics, love, and dark magic intertwine. In Six Wild Crowns, Holly Race breathes new life into the legendary wives of the Tudor king, as clever Boleyn and reluctant queen Seymour forge an unlikely alliance. Amidst treachery and intrigue, they must unite all six queens to save their kingdom from collapse, all while navigating the treacherous desires of a power-hungry monarch. With lush world-building, complex characters, and sapphic yearnings, this stunning novel is a must-read for fans of Sarah J. Maas, Game of Thrones, and Circe.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. The Knight and the Moth

    Rachel Gillig

    In this entrancing romantasy, young prophetess Sybil Delling is forced on a perilous quest with the one knight whose fate remains shrouded in mystery, a dark-eyed, sharp-tongued heretic named Rodrick. When Sybil's fellow Diviners vanish, she must team with Rodrick to unravel the enigma plaguing the kingdom of Traum, facing gods and heresy along the way in this sensational, gothic tale of fate, destiny, and forbidden attraction.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. The Unicorn Hunters A Novel

    Katherine Arden

    This captivating historical fantasy weaves a lush, immersive world around a daring young duchess navigating treacherous politics and ancient magic, with a unique twist on a real historical figure.

    For: Fans of immersive, lyrical fantasy and historical fiction will relish this epic standalone novel.

    Strengths: Arden masterfully blends the allure of mythical creatures with the intrigue of medieval politics, crafting a rich, detailed setting and a compelling protagonist.

    Skip if: Some readers may find the dense historical and cultural context overwhelming, requiring a strong attention to detail.

    Comparable: For those who enjoy historical fantasies with strong female leads, such as Naomi Novik's Uprooted or Sarah J. Maas's Crescent City series, The Unicorn Hunters offers a similarly immersive and enchanting experience.

    “I loved every moment I spent in this magical, dangerous, and haunted realm in the company of its clever and captivating duchess.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. Arcana Academy

    Elise Kova

    In this enchanting fantasy romance debut, tarot card wielder Clara Graysword is rescued from prison by enigmatic Prince Kaelis, who proposes they engage in a ruse at the elite Arcana Academy: she'll be his fake fiancée and help him steal a legendary tarot card. But as they navigate a world of magic and royal intrigue, their facades may crumble, revealing a deeper attraction that could change the course of history. Dive into this captivating series starter to follow Clara's perilous path and discover the secrets hidden within the Academy's ancient walls.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. Blood Over Bright Haven A Novel

    M. L. Wang

    Dive into the dark fantasy world of Blood Over Bright Haven, where ambitious highmage Sciona unravels a hidden conspiracy with the unlikely aid of a mysterious janitor, whose own troubled past holds the key to upending magic's powerful status quo. With intricate worldbuilding, unflinching societal commentary, and a gripping plot, M. L. Wang's standalone novel promises to captivate fans of R.F. Kuang's Babel and anyone drawn to thought-provoking fantasy.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. Violet Thistlewaite Is Not a Villain Anymore

    Emily Krempholtz

    In this enchanting debut, a reformed plant witch and a skeptical alchemist must put aside their differences to save their quaint town from a mysterious plague. As Violet Thistlewaite tries to bloom into a new, kinder self, her blossoming romance with Nathaniel Marsh adds warmth to her quest. With magical secrets, charming characters, and a whimsical setting, this cozy fantasy romance blooms with heart, humor, and romance, inviting readers to cheer on Violet's journey towards redemption and love.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. Blaize and the Maven The Energetics, #1

    Ellen Bard

    In this electrifying begins to a series, Blaize, a powerful but inexperienced energetic, travels to Canada to train with the enigmatic Maven Cuinn. As they navigate ancient magic, family secrets, and their own hidden desires, their tentative bond evolves into a passionate partnership that holds the key to the energetics' fate. Dive into the captivating world of Blaize and the Maven, where romance, magic, and loyalty ignite a thrilling adventure.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. Wild Reverence A Novel

    Rebecca Ross

    Wild Reverence is a captivating, lush tale of forbidden love between a mortal and a goddess, set against the backdrop of a richly detailed world of gods and politics.

    For: Fans of fantasy romance, particularly those who enjoy stories with divine characters and intricate world-building, will appreciate this novel.

    Strengths: Ross's vivid prose and well-developed characters make the fantastical setting come alive, while the slow-burn romance and high-stakes plot keep readers engaged.

    Skip if: Some readers may find the pacing a bit uneven, with moments of intense action alternating with character development and relationship building.

    Comparable: Similar in tone and style to other fantasy novels featuring divine characters, such as Sarah J. Maas's Throne of Glass series or Holly Black's Folk of the Air trilogy, Wild Reverence stands out with its unique blend of mythology and romance.

    “Full of visceral descriptions, expert pacing, and well-timed humor, what starts as a coming-of-age story transforms into a high-stakes game of power.”

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  11. This Kingdom Will Not Kill Me

    Ilona Andrews

    Transport yourself to a gritty, magic-fueled world inspired by Ilona Andrews' signature blend of politics, romance, and high-stakes adventure. When Maggie materializes naked in a back alley, her only lifeline is her exhaustive knowledge of the fictional city of Kair Toren, drawn from years of obsessive reading. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of warlords, assassins, and mythical creatures, Maggie finds herself entwined in the fates of these living characters, all while racing to prevent a catastrophic war. If you devour epic fantasy and portal fantasies like Game of Thrones and Outlander, dive into the captivating world of This Kingdom Will Not Kill Me, where the lines between reality and fiction blur in the most thrilling ways.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  12. The Incandescent

    Emily Tesh

    In this dark academia fantasy reminiscent of Naomi Novik and Plain Bad Heroines, Doctor Walden, a masterful yet troubled magician, must protect Chetwood School from internal demons - literally and figuratively. With its blend of magic, complex characters, and eerie setting, The Incandescent is a captivating read for fans of fantasy and literary fiction, offering a unique and unsettling tale of power, responsibility, and the monsters that lurk within.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  13. Legends & Lattes A Novel of High Fantasy and Low Stakes

    Travis Baldree

    Dive into the delightful world of Legends & Lattes, where fierce warrior Vikings and cunning sorcerers trade in their battleaxes for barista aprons. Author Travis Baldree's charming novel is a masterclass in blending high fantasy with low-stakes humor, creating an irresistible tale that will transport you to a cozy coffeehouse filled with wondrous creatures and warm, witty characters. Get ready to savor every whimsical page, feeling your worries melt away like steamed milk into a perfectly crafted latte.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  14. Kill All Wizards

    Jedediah Berry

    In Jedediah Berry's dark fantasy, a barbarian seeks vengeance against the manipulative wizards who stole his sword and exploited his strength. Blending in with the enemy, he navigates the decadent world of high society to systematically eliminate his foes, a thrilling and unpredictable tale of hatred, revenge, and the clash of cultures.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  15. Night Witch A Spicy Magical Dark Academia Forbidden Romantasy

    Jaymin Eve

    A captivating sequel with a steamy enemies-to-lovers plot, high-stakes magical action, and a rich, dark academic setting.

    For: Fans of paranormal romance, urban fantasy, and dark academia will devour this intense, sensual story.

    Strengths: Intense, forbidden romance between sworn enemies; vivid magical world-building; high-pressure, action-packed plot.

    Skip if: Requires prior knowledge of the Spellcaster series to fully appreciate the complex characters and nuanced world.

    Comparable: Readers of books like Blood of Hercules, The All Souls Trilogy, and the Throne of Glass series will appreciate Night Witch's blend of dark fantasy, romance, and magical academia.

  16. No Ordinary Duke The Crawfords, #1

    Sophie Barnes

    Embark on a charming romance with No Ordinary Duke, where a reluctant duke, Caleb Crawford, disguises himself as a laborer to escape his new title and responsibilities. When he seeks shelter with three spinster friends, including the beautiful and wary Mary Clemens, their undeniable attraction sets in motion a tale of forbidden love and the power of second chances.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  17. Ash Princess

    Laura Sebastian

    In this darkly enchanting tale, Theodosia, a young woman once crowned Ash Princess, must overcome unimaginable hardships and a ruthless occupier to reclaim her throne and free her enslaved people. When forced to commit a terrible act, Theo discovers her sharpest weapon lies in her keen intellect, illuminating a path to power that doesn't rely solely on violence. A smart, feminist retelling of a fallen heroine's journey, Ash Princess is a page-turning epic that will enthrall fans of Victoria Aveyard and Sabaa Tahir until its gripping conclusion.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  18. Rose in Chains

    Julie Soto

    Rose in Chains is a captivating, dark romantic fantasy with a gripping enemies-to-lovers narrative and vivid world-building, making it a standout in the genre.

    For: Fans of complex, morally ambiguous stories and forbidden romances will devour this.

    Strengths: Soto masterfully weaves together a lush, atmospheric setting, intricate magic system, and an intense, emotionally charged romance.

    Skip if: Some readers may find the dense, layered storytelling and dark themes overwhelming.

    Comparable: For those who enjoy similarly dark, romantic fantasies like Stephanie Garber's Caraval series or Leigh Bardugo's Grishaverse, Rose in Chains is a must-read.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  19. Princess Academy graphic novel

    Shannon Hale

    Join Miri on a thrilling journey as she navigates the challenges of Princess Academy, transforming from a humble quarry girl to a potential princess. With breathtaking illustrations and Shannon Hale's captivating storytelling, this graphic novel adaptation brings the beloved classic to vivid life, exploring themes of identity, ambition, and the importance of staying true to oneself amidst great change. Perfect for fans of all ages seeking an immersive, emotionally resonant tale of self-discovery.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  20. Blood & Betrayals

    Jeanette Rose

    Blood & Betrayals is a captivating dark academia tale that masterfully weaves together romance, magic, and mystery, drawing readers into the ancient halls of Avalon University.

    For: Fans of the Zodiac Academy and Atlas Six series will find a compelling new entry in this genre.

    Strengths: The book excels at crafting a rich, immersive world and complex characters, particularly Summer's journey from emotional walls to vulnerability.

    Skip if: The pacing can be slow in parts as the author develops the intricate plot and character relationships.

    Comparable: Readers of dark academia romantas like The All Souls Trilogy and The Magicians series will appreciate Blood & Betrayals' blend of magic, mystery, and forbidden romance.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  21. A Curse So Dark and Lonely

    Brigid Kemmerer

    In a lush and dark retelling of Beauty and the Beast, Prince Rhen is cursed to repeat his 18th year, doomed to turn into a destructive beast at autumn's end. When Harper, a scrappy and underestimated teen, is thrust into Rhen's enchanted world, she brings a spark of hope. As they navigate the curse and sinister forces threatening Emberfall, they must also confront the lies and secrets that trap them both. With its fresh take on a classic tale and a forbidden romance that crackles with intensity, A Curse So Dark and Lonely is a must-read for fantasy and YA fans.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  22. Adversary to the Villain A Cozy Fantasy Romantic Comedy

    Hannah Nicole Maehrer

    Oh my gosh, have you read 'Adversary to the Villain'? It's such a delightful cozy fantasy that will transport you to a whimsical world of magic, mayhem, and romance! Perfect for fans of witty banter, lovable misfits, and slow-burn relationships, this charming novel follows a reluctant hero and her stubborn nemesis as they navigate a war of wits and eventually discover a shared destiny. With its lighthearted humor, unforgettable characters, and satisfying resolution, it's the ideal escape for anyone seeking a humorous, heartwarming tale with a dash of fantasy fun!

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  23. Lady Danger The Warrior Maids of Rivenloch, #1

    Glynnis Campbell

    Embark on a thrilling adventure as Deirdre, a fearless warrior, navigates love and loyalty in Lady Danger, the first installment of Glynnis Campbell's The Warrior Maids of Rivenloch series. When Deirdre tricks the admirable knight Sir Pagan into a marriage of convenience, their initial clash of wills soon ignites a passionate connection. Prepare to be captivated by this rousing tale of strong willed heroines, valiant heroes, and the unforgettable romances that blossom in the face of danger and destiny.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  24. The Assassin's Blade

    Scott Marlowe

    Immerse yourself in a gritty, gas-lit world where treachery lurks around every corner. Meet the enigmatic Assassin Without a Name, a master of discretion and deadly skill who finds himself entangled in a perilous web of intrigue. Alongside his former flame, the cunning thief Elizabeth West, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of warring factions, secret societies, and dark magic to unravel a conspiracy threatening the very city. Will their unlikely alliance survive, or will the Assassin's choice seal their fates? Dive into this rich, complex fantasy for a gripping ride filled with morally nuanced characters, dark technology, and ancient magic.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  25. Back for Blood: Never Whistle at Night Part II Shane Hawk (Edited by), Theodore C. Van Alst Jr. (Edited by)

    Shane Hawk

    "Prepare for a chilling descent into the dark heart of horror with Back for Blood: Never Whistle at Night Part II. This stellar anthology showcases twenty-one gripping, unsettling Indigenous tales of supernatural terror and everyday horrors, expertly curated by Shane Hawk and Theodore Van Alst Jr. From sinister psychological mindgames to savage monster hunts, each story is a masterful blend of unsettling terror and unflinching honesty about living under colonial rule. Dive in for a gut-wrenching, spine-tingling ride that will leave you craving more of the bold, unflinching voice of Indigenous dark fiction."

  26. The Southern Sorority of Superstitious Witches A Novel

    Katie Ortiz

    In post-Civil War Alabama, three women join a trial semester at a prestigious university, each with their own reasons to fight sexism and educational discrimination. When pranks escalate, Lucille, Catherine, and Victoria form a witch coven, drawing on Southern folklore magic to defend themselves. But beneath the university's surface, a darker force threatens to expel all women. Dive into this spellbinding Southern Gothic novel, perfect for fans of Hester Fox and Louisa Morgan, as the women must navigate oppressive society and survive through cunning, magic, and the power of their own bonds.
