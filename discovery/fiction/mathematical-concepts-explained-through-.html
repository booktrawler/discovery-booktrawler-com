Start here

If you only read one series delving into mathematical concepts through captivating stories, make it Solvej Balle's 'On the Calculation of Volume' quintet.

Step into the captivating world of advanced mathematics through Solvej Balle's acclaimed 'On the Calculation of Volume' series. By masterfully intertwining mathematical concepts with vivid storytelling, Balle creates a unique reading experience that appeals to both math enthusiasts and those seeking a compelling narrative. This award-nominated series, consisting of five interconnected books, delves into complex theories and applications, making abstract ideas more accessible and engaging for readers of all backgrounds.

The collection

  1. On the Calculation of Volume (Book I): Shortlisted for the 2025 International Booker Prize

    Solvej Balle

    On the Calculation of Volume is a mesmerizing, philosophical exploration of time, love, and existence, expertly weaving a tragic, hopeful narrative through its innovative structure.

    For: Literary fiction enthusiasts, fans of complex, psychologically driven stories, and readers interested in philosophical themes.

    Strengths: Masterful storytelling, hypnotic prose, and a unique, recursive narrative structure that deepens the emotional impact of the protagonist's plight.

    Skip if: The dense, poetic writing style may not be for everyone, requiring close attention and patience to fully absorb.

    Comparable: Readers who appreciate novels like Slaughterhouse-Five or The Time Traveler's Wife will find similarities in their unconventional approaches to time and narrative.

    “A sober, thoughtful study of time and connection.”

  2. On the Calculation of Volume (Book II)

    Solvej Balle

    On the Calculation of Volume is a thought-provoking, lyrical exploration of time, identity, and perception, with vivid, sensory writing that immerses readers in its protagonist's hypnotic experience.

    For: Literary fiction enthusiasts, fans of philosophical and psychological themes, and those who appreciate lyrical, evocative prose.

    Strengths: Balle's masterful use of language to evoke a heightened sense of the mundane, Tara's poignant struggle to maintain her sense of self amidst temporal stasis, and the novel's thought-provoking musings on the nature of time and identity.

    Skip if: This is a dense, complex work that rewards close reading; some may find Tara's fixation on the same day challenging to relate to.

    Comparable: Readers who appreciate experimental, introspective fiction akin to works by authors like Virginia Woolf, Thomas Pynchon, or Don DeLillo may find On the Calculation of Volume's unique blend of philosophical inquiry and poetic prose appealing.

    “These books are the talk of the town in New York right now (or at least in my New York).--Kaitlin Phillips”

  3. On the Calculation of Volume (Book III)

    Solvej Balle

    On the Calculation of Volume is a philosophical, poetic novel that explores the human condition through a unique premise of a perpetual autumn day, raising existential questions about time, reality, and human connection.

    For: Fans of literary fiction, philosophy, and those interested in introspective, thought-provoking storytelling.

    Strengths: Balle's hypnotic, repetitive prose creates an immersive atmosphere, while her piercing attentiveness renders the protagonist's experiences endlessly intriguing. The novel blends metaphysical inquiry with intimate domestic scenes.

    Skip if: The dense, conceptual nature may deter readers seeking a fast-paced narrative.

    Comparable: Readers who appreciate novels that merge philosophy with everyday life, such as Thomas Mann's 'The Magic Mountain' or Albert Camus's 'The Stranger', will find similar themes and styles in Solvej Balle's work.

    “Solvej Balle is a prodigious writer who, miraculously, finds the subtlest, most fascinating differences in repetition.”

  4. On the Calculation of Volume (Book IV)

    Solvej Balle

    On the Calculation of Volume (Book IV) is a captivating literary experiment that reimagines the concept of time and community, with profound philosophical implications.

    For: Fans of avant-garde fiction, literary theory enthusiasts, and anyone intrigued by the intersection of language, identity, and social dynamics.

    Strengths: Balle's inventive storytelling, vivid characterizations, and thought-provoking themes make for a rich, immersive reading experience.

  5. On the Calculation of Volume (Book V)

    Solvej Balle

    Solvej Balle's innovative septology masterfully explores existential questions, philosophical musings, and the human condition through Tara's hypnotic diary entries.

    For: Literary fiction enthusiasts, fans of experimental storytelling, and those seeking profound reflections on life, loss, and time.

Frequently asked questions

What makes Solvej Balle's series unique?

Are the books part of a series or standalone?
The 'On the Calculation of Volume' series consists of five interconnected books, each building upon the previous one to create a comprehensive exploration of mathematical theories and applications.
