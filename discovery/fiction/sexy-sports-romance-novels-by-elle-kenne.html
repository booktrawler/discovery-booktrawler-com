Sexy Sports Romance Novels by Elle Kennedy

Elle Kennedy's books, including 'The Score', deliver steamy, humorous, and emotive tales of athletes and artists.

Who these books are for

  • Romance readers seeking sexy, comedic, and emotionally resonant stories
  • Fans of sports and athlete protagonists
  • Readers looking for lighthearted, feel-good love stories with heat

Start here

If you only read one sexy sports romance, choose 'The Score' for its captivating hockey star hero and delightful, passionate love story.

Elle Kennedy's sexy sports romances have captivated readers worldwide with their unique blend of humor, heart, and steamy passion. From the ice of professional hockey to the fast-paced world of football, Kennedy's athletes and artists embark on captivating journeys of self-discovery, love, and triumph. With her signature wit, relatable characters, and sensual storytelling, Kennedy weaves unforgettable tales that will leave you cheering for more.

Frequently asked questions

Is Elle Kennedy's writing suitable for all ages?
While the themes and content are generally mature, Kennedy's stories often balance steamy scenes with humor and heartfelt relationships suitable for new adult and adult readers.
What genres do Elle Kennedy's sports romance novels fall under?
Kennedy's books combine elements of new adult, contemporary romance, and sports fiction, with a focus on the passionate, developing relationships between athletes and artists.
Are Elle Kennedy's sports romances based on real sports or athletes?
While the characters and teams are fictional, Kennedy often incorporates authentic sports details, tactics, and atmospheres to immerse readers in the worlds of hockey, football, and more.
Can I read Elle Kennedy's sports romances as standalone novels?
Most of Kennedy's sports romances can be enjoyed independently, although some share overlapping characters and settings, creating a cozy, connected series feel.
