Essential Young Adult Science Fiction Series

Dive into the thrilling Animorphs series by K.A. Applegate, where teens discover the power to morph into animals to fight alien invaders.

Who these books are for

  • Teens seeking exciting sci-fi & fantasy
  • Parents looking for age-appropriate YA fiction
  • Fans of action, adventure, and coming-of-age stories

Start here

If you only read one iconic YA sci-fi series, choose Animorphs for its unique blend of animal morphing, alien battles, and relatable teen protagonists.

Imagine having the power to transform into any animal, at will. This extraordinary ability is the heart of the Animorphs series by K.A. Applegate. When five ordinary teens stumble upon an alien spacecraft, they're chosen to become the world's only defense against the invaders. As they struggle to balance school, family, and their newfound morphing abilities, they must also confront the horrors of war and the blurred lines between good and evil. With its unique blend of science fiction, fantasy, and coming-of-age themes, Animorphs has captivated readers for generations and remains a touchstone of young adult literature.

The collection

  1. Animorphs #1: The Invasion

    K. A. Applegate

    The Animorphs series has captivated readers for decades with its blend of science fiction, action, and coming-of-age themes, making it a valuable addition to any collection.

    For: Middle grade and young adult readers, as well as nostalgic adult fans, will enjoy this relaunch of the beloved series.

    Strengths: The Invasion delivers pulse-pounding adventure, memorable characters, and an engaging premise that explores the complexities of being a hero.

    Skip if: Some readers may find the pacing a bit slow as the story sets up the world and characters, but this pays off in the long run with a rich, immersive tale.

    Comparable: Fans of The Hunger Games, Divergent, and other dystopian series will appreciate Animorphs' similar themes of rebellion against an oppressive force.

  2. Animorphs #2: The Visitor

    K. A. Applegate

    A gripping sci-fi adventure that introduces the Animorphs' secret mission to save humanity from parasitic invasion.

    For: Young adult readers, fans of action-packed speculative fiction, and anyone drawn to stories about friendship and courage in the face of extraordinary challenges.

    Strengths: Compelling characters, relentless pacing, and a gripping premise that explores themes of identity, loyalty, and the moral complexities of espionage and warfare.

    Skip if: Some readers may find the episodic structure and cliffhanger ending frustrating if they're not already invested in the series.

    Comparable: Fans of The Hunger Games, Divergent, and other dystopian YA series will appreciate the Animorphs' blend of action, strategy, and interpersonal drama in a high-stakes, alternate reality.

Frequently asked questions

What is the Animorphs series about?
Teens discover they can morph into animals and must use this power to fight invading alien forces.
Who is the target audience?
Young adults, ages 12-18, and fans of science fiction and adventure.
How many books are in the series?
The original series consists of 54 books, plus several spin-offs and graphic novels.
Are the books suitable for younger readers?
While the series is geared towards teens, younger readers with mature tastes may also enjoy it.
