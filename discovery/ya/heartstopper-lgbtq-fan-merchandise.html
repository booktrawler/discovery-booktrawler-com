Start here

If you only collect one Heartstopper item, the enamel pin set captures the characters' essence.

Heartstopper has captured the hearts of readers worldwide with its authentic portrayal of queer relationships and the challenges of adolescence. For fans eager to show their devotion, this selection of official merchandise provides a range of ways to express their enthusiasm. From functional items like tote bags and keychains to decorative accessories like enamel pins and a bookmark, there's something for every aspect of a fan's life. Whether you're looking for a unique gift or a way to celebrate your own love for the series, this collection offers a variety of options to connect with the Heartstopper community.

Frequently asked questions

What is Heartstopper?
A popular graphic novel series by Alice Oseman exploring LGBTQ+ relationships and coming-of-age themes.
Are all these items official merchandise?
Yes, they are licensed and officially released by Out of Print, in collaboration with the creators.
Are there more Heartstopper items available?
This curated collection showcases some of the most iconic and popular merchandise. The series and its fan base continue to grow, so new items may emerge in the future.
