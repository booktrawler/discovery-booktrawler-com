Cookbooks for Teenagers

Get your teenager excited about cooking with these engaging and easy-to-follow cookbooks.

Who these books are for

  • Teenagers learning to cook
  • Parents seeking fun family recipes
  • Young adults wanting to expand their culinary skills

Start here

If you only read one cookbook with your teenager, make it America's Test Kitchen's 'The Complete Cookbook for Teen Chefs'.

Teach your teenager the joy of cooking with these engaging, easy-to-follow cookbooks. Perfect for beginners, these international recipe collections emphasize balanced ingredients and fun presentation. Whether you're cooking with a teen or an adult looking to expand their culinary skills, these books provide a delightful culinary journey.

The collection

  1. The Complete Cookbook for Teen Chefs 70+ Teen-Tested and Teen-Approved Recipes to Cook, Eat and Share

    America's Test Kitchen Kids

    This comprehensive cookbook stands out for its rigorous testing and kid-approved recipes, offering a wide variety of dishes to appeal to teen chefs' adventurous palates.

    For: Teenagers and young adults who are eager to learn cooking skills and explore global cuisine.

    Strengths: The book's greatest strengths are its well-tested, accessible recipes and the emphasis on fundamental kitchen techniques and safety practices, making it an excellent resource for novice teen cooks.

    Skip if: While the book is visually appealing, some recipes may still require more supervision and hands-on guidance from an adult, especially for younger teens.

    Comparable: Other cookbooks for young people, such as 'The Kids' Cookbook' by Anna Ginsburg and 'The Junior Chef Collection' by Phoebe Wood, offer similar hands-on learning and globally inspired recipes, but with a slightly more beginner-focused approach.

Frequently asked questions

Are these cookbooks suitable for beginners?
Yes, all the books in this collection are designed for novice cooks and include clear instructions.
Do they focus on specific cuisines?
The collection spans various international flavors, from classic American to global favorites.
Are the recipes healthy?
While the books do include some indulgent treats, they emphasize balanced, wholesome ingredients and cooking methods.
Can adults enjoy these cookbooks too?
Absolutely! The engaging presentation and accessible recipes make them enjoyable for cooks of all ages.
