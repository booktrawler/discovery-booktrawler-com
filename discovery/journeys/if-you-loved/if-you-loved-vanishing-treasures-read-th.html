If You Loved Vanishing Treasures, Read These 8 Captivating Books

Immerse yourself in enchanting stories of hidden worlds, adventurous quests, and the power of imagination with these spellbinding reads.

Who these books are for

  • Fans of whimsical, imaginative storytelling
  • Readers who enjoy hidden world and secret society themes
  • Adventurous spirits seeking thrilling quests and puzzles to solve

Start here

If you only read one, choose The Lost Bookshop - its nostalgic charm and magical bookstore setting make it a perfect companion to Vanishing Treasures.

Vanishing Treasures captivates readers with its intricate plot, rich world-building, and captivating characters. If you're craving more of the same magic, look no further! This curated list of eight enchanting books offers a delightful mix of fantasy, adventure, and mystery that will transport you to captivating realms and keep you on the edge of your seat. From hidden libraries to secret societies, these spellbinding stories are sure to satisfy your appetite for imaginative tales.

The collection

  1. The Lost Bookshop

    Evie Woods

    Step into the enchanting world of The Lost Bookshop, where the magic of storytelling brings together three unlikely souls. Opaline, Martha, and Henry's ordinary lives are forever changed when they stumble upon a mysterious Dublin bookshop that seems to vanish and reappear at will. As they unravel the secrets hidden within its shelves, they become embroiled in a captivating tale that blurs the lines between reality and fantasy. With its beautifully woven prose and nods to classic literature, this charming novel is a must-read for book lovers everywhere, promising to sweep you away on a journey of self-discovery and the transformative power of reading.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. Lost and Found Jigsaw Book

    Oliver Jeffers

    Indulge in a heartwarming tale of unlikely friendship as a curious boy and an adventurous penguin navigate the ups and downs of companionship. With Oliver Jeffers' endearing storytelling and delightful illustrations, this charming picture book is a must-read for kids of all ages. Now, take the immersive experience to another level by completing the six included 12-piece jigsaw puzzles, bringing each whimsical scene to vivid life.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. The Poisoned King

    Katherine Rundell

    "Embark on a daring adventure in The Poisoned King, the enthralling sequel to Impossible Creatures. When a rare dragon summons Christopher Forrester to mythic Archipelago, he finds a world plagued by poison. With the help of fierce heroine Anya and an unforgettable cast of magical creatures, plunge into a perilous quest for justice - featuring 50 stunning illustrations that bring the fantastical to life. A thrilling must-read for young and old alike!"

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. The Ballad of Falling Dragons

    Sarah A Parker

    Immerse yourself in the breathtaking world of The Ballad of Falling Dragons, where ancient magic, mysterious creatures, and an unforgettable love story collide. This New York Times #1 bestseller and eagerly anticipated sequel to When the Moon Hatched promises a thrilling adventure as Raeve navigates vengeance, forbidden romance, and the fate of realms. With daring quests, burning secrets, and the promise of a world-changing revelation, this epic fantasy romance will captivate and leave you yearning for the next moonfall. Pick up this instant classic to be swept into a mesmerizing tale of passion, power, and self-discovery.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. Lord of the Flies

    William Golding

    Stranded on a remote island with no adults, a group of schoolboys descend into chaos and darkness, revealing the shadowy depths of human nature. William Golding's timeless classic, Lord of the Flies, is a gripping tale of survival, fear, and the thin line between civilization and savagery. This thought-provoking novel, adapted into a Netflix series, remains a must-read for its profound exploration of the human condition.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. The Fragile Threads of Power

    V. E. Schwab

    In this electrifying new adventure from V.E. Schwab, magic and danger collide across three Londons, threatening to unravel the fragile balance of power. Renegade thief Delilah Bard, child queen Kosika, and mysterious Tes must navigate treacherous alliances, ancient prophecies, and the dark allure of each world before they succumb to the very forces that seek to destroy them. With old friends and foes alike at risk, Schwab masterfully weaves a thrilling tale of magic, intrigue, and the unbreakable bonds that forge heroes and legends.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. The Man Who Died Twice A Thursday Murder Club Mystery

    Richard Osman

    Laugh-out-loud fun and clever mystery abound in this second installment of the Thursday Murder Club series from Richard Osman. Join Elizabeth, Joyce, Ron, and Ibrahim as they once again become amateur sleuths, this time dealing with a diamond heist and a ruthless killer. With their quick wit, charm, and experience, will the septuagenarian quartet catch the culprit before it's too late? Dive into this unputdownable, delightful read for an unforgettable detective adventure filled with humor and heart.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. Haven and the Fallen Giants

    Jen Xu

    Join Haven, an inventive desert outcast, as she navigates a harsh world of prejudice and survival. When she stumbles upon a powerful relic, it unleashes a sleeping giant and sets off an epic adventure with her unlikely ally Lan. This vibrant, full-color graphic novel combines dystopian futurism, fantasy, and coming-of-age themes, perfect for fans of Nimona, Wings of Fire, and Avatar the Last Airbender. Get ready for a thrilling ride filled with action, steamy romance, and unexpected friendship that will keep you turning the pages.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. The Broken Earth Trilogy The Fifth Season, the Obelisk Gate, the Stone Sky

    N K Jemisin

    Dive into the breathtaking world of the Broken Earth, where seismic events reshape the landscape and the very fabric of society. N.K. Jemisin's stunning trilogy weaves together threads of myth, oppression, and elemental power in a richly imagined fantasy realm. With each installment building on the previous, this collectible boxed set delivers a gripping, award-winning narrative that will captivate you from start to finish.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. The Language of Thorns Midnight Tales and Dangerous Magic

    Leigh Bardugo

    Dive into the darkly enchanting world of the Grishaverse with Leigh Bardugo's The Language of Thorns, a mesmerizing collection of six spellbinding tales of magic, betrayal, and love. From haunting forests to haunted towns, these lavishly illustrated stories transport you to a realm both familiar and strange, where the lines between good and evil blur and the price of desire can be deadly. Perfect for both newcomers and dedicated fans alike, Bardugo's masterful storytelling weaves a web of unforgettable characters and worlds, each meticulously crafted to leave you breathless. Enter this alluring realm of thorns and language where the true magic lies in the tales themselves.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

Frequently asked questions

What if I don't like fantasy? Can I still enjoy these books?
Yes, while fantasy elements are prominent, the stories also delve into real-world themes, character development, and emotional journeys that will resonate with readers of all genres.
Are these books suitable for children or young adults?
Some titles are geared towards younger audiences, but several are geared towards adult readers as well. The suitability depends on the individual child's or reader's taste and maturity level.
Do I need to read these books in order?
No, each book is a standalone tale, so you can enjoy them in any sequence without worrying about continuity or spoilers.
