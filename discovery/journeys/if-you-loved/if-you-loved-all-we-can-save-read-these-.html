If You Loved All We Can Save, Read These 10 Books

For an empowering blend of personal stories, scientific insights, and activist guidance, these volumes offer a next-step reading journey.

Who these books are for

  • Environmental activists and organizers
  • Concerned citizens seeking personal and policy insights
  • Book clubs discussing environmental issues

Start here

If you only read one, choose 'The Uninhabitable Earth' for a gripping, science-driven exploration of climate change's global impact.

Building on the inspiration of 'All We Can Save', these ten books dive deeper into the climate crisis, personal activism, and paths forward. From compelling memoirs to groundbreaking scientific reports, they offer a multifaceted reading journey perfect for environmentalists seeking more knowledge, motivation, and practical tools.

The collection

  1. Turtles All the Way Down

    John Green

    In this profound and deeply relatable novel, John Green shines a light on the complexity of the teenage experience, particularly for Aza Holmes, who grapples with the challenges of mental illness, friendship, and first love. With raw honesty and exquisite prose, Green crafts an unforgettable story that illuminates the beauty and resilience of the human spirit, reminding us that even in the darkest moments, connection and hope can prevail.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. We Love You An Optimistic Guide to Life on a Rock Floating Through Space

    Thomas Sullivan

    Embark on a thought-provoking journey with lifelong friends Andy Min and Thomas Sullivan as they explore humanity's relationship with nature, self, and the vast universe we inhabit. Through a mix of poetic prose, narrative, and stunning visuals, We Love You inspires readers to reexamine life's big questions and find meaning, joy, and hope in the everyday world we often take for granted. With its optimistic spirit and heartfelt wisdom, this National Bestseller is a must-read for anyone seeking a deeper connection to the world and themselves.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. Democracy or Else How to Save America in 10 Easy Steps

    Jon Favreau

    From the creators of Pod Save America comes this timely, illustrated guide to taking action and making a difference in American politics. With a mix of humor and practical advice, "Democracy or Else" breaks down the complex process into 10 manageable steps for getting informed, donating, volunteering, organizing, and more. Featuring insights from savvy experts and refreshingly down-to-earth politicians, this book is the perfect resource for political newcomers and seasoned activists alike, empowering readers to join the fight to save democracy in the face of unprecedented challenges and contradictions.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. What We Can Know A Novel

    Ian McEwan

    From Booker Prize-winner Ian McEwan comes an astonishing novel that spans centuries, delving into the mysteries of human connection and the power of words. Through the lens of a lost poem, What We Can Know weaves together stories of love, betrayal, and the blurred lines between past and future. With McEwan's trademark mastery, this timeless tale explores what we gain by chasing secrets and the enduring legacy of our relationships. A captivating literary journey that will leave you pondering the depths of human knowledge.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. You Matter

    Christian Robinson

    Celebrate the interconnectedness of all beings with Christian Robinson's radiant You Matter. Through luminous illustrations and a thoughtful, empathetic perspective, this Caldecott Honoree invites young readers to see the world from diverse viewpoints and understand that every individual is vital and valuable, no matter their size or role. A powerful and eye-opening read perfect for nurturing compassion and global understanding.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. You Can Change the World

    Brad Meltzer

    Inspirational quotes and vibrant artwork from the acclaimed Ordinary People Change the World series come together in this uplifting graduation gift, featuring the courageous wisdom of trailblazers like Amelia Earhart, Albert Einstein, and Martin Luther King Jr. Perfect for heroes-in-training, it's a fun, friendly book to cherish and share.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. This Story Might Save Your Life A Novel

    Tiffany Crum

    "Get ready for a heart-pounding thrill ride! Tiffany Crum weaves a gripping tale of best friends Benny and Joy, podcast hosts who share harrowing survival stories. But when Joy vanishes, Benny is accused of murder, and a sinister web of secrets from their past threatens to unravel their empire. A riveting mystery, tender romance, and pulse-defining suspense make this instant NYT bestseller a must-read for fans of pulse-pounding page-turners."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. Courage Can Save Us Ten Extraordinary Americans and the Fight for Our Future

    Rye Barcott

    Meet 9 principled veterans and a former FBI agent who are charting a courageous path in public life, defying political polarization and setting an inspiring example. In "Courage Can Save Us", Rye Barcott shares the stories of these trailblazers as they navigate the moral and personal costs of leadership, exposing the darkness of negativity and outrage in favor of service and the common good. A timely, rousing call to action for a more united America.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. Before I Let Go

    Kennedy Ryan

    Rediscover the love that once seemed destined to last forever in Kennedy Ryan's impassioned second chance romance, Before I Let Go. When Yasmen and Josiah Wade are drawn back together after a decade apart, their rekindled attraction ignites a deep longing to reclaim their relationship. But the scars of their painful past threaten to tear them asunder once more. A heart-wrenching, intensely sensual journey that probes the depths of love, forgiveness, and second chances, Before I Let Go is a triumph from a New York Times bestseller sure to leave readers breathless.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. Unsettling Salad!

    Aaron Reynolds

    Join Jasper Rabbit as he shares the chilling tale of Thadeus Badger and the broccoli that bites back! When this typical badger makes a salad-eating deal, he learns the hard way that even the healthiest foods can have a dark side. With humor and fright, this creepy chapter book will leave you on edge, warning you to beware of veggies that can turn the tables.

    Buy on Bookshop.org
