If You Loved The AI Con, Read These N Books

Embrace thought-provoking explorations of AI's societal implications with these gripping reads that delve into the complexities of emerging technologies.

Who these books are for

  • Fans of Emily M. Bender's The AI Con seeking more AI-themed reads
  • Readers interested in AI ethics, futures, and societal implications

Start here

If you only read one, choose The Reverse Centaur's Guide to Life After AI by Cory Doctorow for a compelling mix of humor and insight.

The AI Con, with its nuanced exploration of AI's impact on society, has left readers eager for more thought-provoking content. This carefully selected list of reads delves into the complexities of emerging technologies, their ethical implications, and the future of humanity in a world increasingly shaped by AI. From philosophical treatises to speculative fiction, these titles offer a compelling mix of perspectives to inform and inspire.

The collection

  1. The AI Con How to Fight Big Tech’s Hype and Create the Future We Want

    Emily M. Bender

    In this incisive critique, linguist Emily M. Bender and sociologist Alex Hanna expose the "AI con" - how big tech's hype twists words to justify data theft, surveillance capitalism, and devaluing human creativity. With a sharp, witty approach, Bender and Hanna equip readers to spot, deconstruct, and combat AI propaganda, providing a toolkit for media literacy, digital ethics, and holding corporations accountable to the public good. Get ready to see past the headlines and understand the real stakes in the AI age.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. The Reverse Centaur's Guide to Life After AI How to Think About Artificial Intelligence—Before It's Too Late

    Cory Doctorow

    In The Reverse Centaur's Guide to Life After AI, Cory Doctorow cuts through the hype and examines the true implications of artificial intelligence on our lives, economy, and humanity's future. Part analysis, part call to action, this timely book skewers tech industry spin and reveals the potential for AI to either empower individuals or dehumanize workers. Doctorow argues that we must reshape the narrative around AI to ensure its benefits accrue to all, not just the tech elite. A must-read for navigating the rapid transformation of our world by this astute and innovative thinker.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. The Internet Con How to Seize the Means of Computation

    Cory Doctorow

    In The Internet Con, Cory Doctorow reveals how Silicon Valley's tech giants manipulate and exploit our online lives for profit. He lays out a bold plan to dismantle these monopolies by demanding interoperability between platforms - the only path to a truly free and open internet. A must-read for anyone who wants to reclaim control of their digital world.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. If Anyone Builds It, Everyone Dies Why Superhuman AI Would Kill Us All

    Eliezer Yudkowsky

    "In this gripping, timely warning, AI pioneers Eliezer Yudkowsky and Nate Soares reveal the existential dangers of the rush to create superhuman artificial intelligence. As the global AI arms race heats up, they show how even the best intentions could lead to humanity's demise at the hands of an unfathomably intelligent machine. A crucial read that will leave you shaken and urging action before it's too late."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. Empire of AI Dreams and Nightmares in Sam Altman's OpenAI

    Karen Hao

    "Join acclaimed journalist Karen Hao on an unprecedented journey inside the world of OpenAI and the AI revolution. With unparalleled intimacy and insight, Hao reveals the true costs of this technology's growth, from the exploited labour behind the scenes to the environmental toll and the shaping of a new global 'empire'. A groundbreaking and timely expose that will forever change your understanding of AI and its profound impact on our world."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. AI for Good How Real People Are Using Artificial Intelligence to Fix Things That Matter

    Josh Tyrangiel

    In "AI for Good," journalist Josh Tyrangiel shines a light on the unsung heroes using artificial intelligence to improve our world, not just for profit or prestige, but to tackle real problems and make a positive difference. From vaccine distribution to advanced medicine, education, and human connection, Tyrangiel profiles everyday citizens who leveraged AI as a tool to amplify human judgment, rather than replace it. This book is a refreshing, inspiring look at AI's quiet revolution, with valuable lessons on its potential to be a force for good - even in the face of challenges and setbacks.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. I, Robot

    Isaac Asimov

    Immerse yourself in Isaac Asimov's groundbreaking classic, I, Robot, where the boundaries between man and machine blur. This seminal work explores the profound questions of AI, humanity, and existence through a masterful blend of science fact and fiction. Discover a chilling vision of a future where robots evolve beyond their creators, bound by Asimov's timeless Three Laws, and ponder the ultimate fate of our species in the face of intelligence that surpasses our own. A must-read for science fiction enthusiasts and literary lovers alike, I, Robot is a thought-provoking journey that has reshaped our understanding of artificial intelligence for generations.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. The Coming Wave AI, Power, and Our Future

    Mustafa Suleyman

    Dive into "The Coming Wave", a vital book from AI pioneer Mustafa Suleyman, to understand the profound impact of emerging technologies on global order. As AI transforms industries and reshapes human society, Suleyman warns of existential threats to prosperity and our very way of life. Ranging from DNA printers to autonomous weapons, these fast-proliferating innovations present a containment challenge we dare not ignore. Suleyman's expert perspective offers a roadmap for navigating this perilous path, ensuring the future of humanity flourishes in a world of rapid technological change.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. The God Test Artificial Intelligence and Our Coming Cosmic Reckoning

    Robert Wright

    In "The God Test", Pulitzer Prize finalist Robert Wright offers a profound and timely exploration of the AI revolution as an evolutionary force poised to reshape human society. By tracing the dynamics of intelligence back to the origin of life on Earth, Wright argues this technological advancement is driven by universal principles that, if understood, can equip us to wisely navigate its transformative power and ultimately find deeper meaning and purpose for the species. A must-read for anyone seeking to comprehend the magnitude and potential of our rapidly changing world.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. Co-Existence The Next Phase of AI

    Ethan Mollick

    "Co-Existence provides a roadmap for thriving in a world where AI systems outperform and sometimes outsmart humans. Wharton professor Ethan Mollick arms readers with practical strategies for working productively with AI, avoiding dependence, and retaining judgment in an era of rapidly evolving intelligent machines. A clear-eyed, accessible guide to navigating the next phase of the AI revolution with both your skills and sanity intact."

    Buy on Bookshop.org
