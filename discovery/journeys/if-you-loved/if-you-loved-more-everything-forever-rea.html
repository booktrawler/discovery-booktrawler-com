If You Loved More Everything Forever, Read These 10 Unforgettable Books

If you adored the whimsical, tender, and heartwarming qualities of More Everything Forever, immerse yourself in these 10 books that share its essence of finding love, connection, and personal growth.

Who these books are for

  • Fans of charming, character-driven novels
  • Readers seeking heartwarming stories of love and growth
  • Anyone looking for whimsical, imaginative fiction

Start here

If you only read one, choose To Kill a Mockingbird – its timeless themes of empathy, kindness, and the power of a good heart make it a quintessential companion to More Everything Forever.

More Everything Forever's enchanting blend of whimsy, heart, and personal growth has captured the hearts of many readers. If you're looking for more books that share its captivating essence, look no further. This curated list of 10 books features stories of love, self-discovery, and the magic of human connection – all set within imaginative worlds and relatable characters. Whether you crave more graphic novels, nostalgic romances, or philosophical insights, these read-alikes promise to transport you to new emotional depths and leave you feeling uplifted and inspired.

The collection

  1. To Kill a Mockingbird

    Harper Lee

    In the sweltering summer of 1935, racial tensions simmer just beneath the surface of Maycomb, Alabama. Against this searing backdrop, Scout Finch learns the harsh realities of justice, innocence, and the unyielding human spirit. Through the eyes of a child, Harper Lee masterfully weaves a timeless tale of moral courage in 'To Kill a Mockingbird', a novel that continues to challenge and inspire generations with its poignant exploration of a community grappling with the shadows of its past.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. Heartstopper #6: A Graphic Novel

    Alice Oseman

    In the poignant, heartfelt conclusion to the beloved Heartstopper series, Alice Oseman masterfully captures the bittersweet beauty of young love and self-discovery. As Charlie pursues head boy and Nick journeys towards college, they must navigate change and uncertain futures while holding fast to the unbreakable bond between them. With unflinching honesty, Oseman tackles tough topics like mental health and eating disorders, crafting a deeply moving and authentic portrayal of teenage life. Don't miss this unforgettable final chapter in the triumphant story of Nick and Charlie.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. It Lasts Forever and Then It's Over

    Anne de Marcken

    In Anne de Marcken's genre-bending novel It Lasts Forever and Then It's Over, a voraciously alive heroine navigates the bizarre landscapes of the afterlife, yearning for the love and humanity she left behind. With a dead but opinionated crow in her chest, she encounters a series of terrifying, hilarious, and heart-wrenching situations, shedding pieces of her body and self along the way. A bold and imaginative tale that probes mortality's transformative power, this acclaimed novel delivers a bracing and unforgettable reading experience.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. The Phantom Tollbooth

    Norton Juster

    Step through the Tollbooth and discover a world where words come alive and even the most mundane day can be transformed into an unforgettable adventure. Join Milo on his whimsical journey as he outwits the watchdog Tock, rescues Rhyme and Reason, and learns that the blandest existence can hold the most exciting secrets. With Jules Feiffer's iconic illustrations illuminating Norton Juster's clever wordplay, The Phantom Tollbooth is a timeless classic that will charm and delight generations of readers.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. Every Summer After

    Carley Fortune

    In Carley Fortune's heartfelt debut, Every Summer After, a decade-old heartbreak propels Percy back to her idyllic lakeside hometown and unexpectedly reunites her with her high school sweetheart, Sam. As they navigate the complexities of their unresolved feelings, this sweeping summer romance delves into the power of second chances, the enduring bonds of youth, and the possibility of reclaiming lost love. Evocative of Emily Henry's style, this nostalgic tale will captivate readers who crave sentimental, small-town stories infused with a touch of wistfulness and a grand, second-chance love story.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. Love You More A Novel

    Emily Giffin

    In this emotionally charged novel, Emily Giffin masterfully weaves together past and present as Billie's seemingly perfect life is upended by an urgent call from her first love, Mick. Jolted back to her Wisconsin roots, Billie must confront the choices that led her away and where her heart truly lies, all while navigating the fragility of love and its power to transform everything. Giffin's gripping tale of love, loss, and second chances makes for an unforgettable read.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. Heartstopper #1-6 Box Set

    Alice Oseman

    Praise for Alice Oseman's Heartstopper series: "Heartwarming, relatable, and utterly charming" - Booklist. This complete 6-volume graphic novel box set follows the tender, authentic romance of Charlie and Nick, two high school boys whose friendship blossoms into something more as they navigate the ups and downs of adolescence. Heartstopper is a poignant portrayal of coming-of-age, mental health, and the complexities of love and identity. A must-read for anyone who's ever felt like an outsider or hoped to find their soulmate.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. All the Colors of the Dark: A Read with Jenna Pick

    Chris Whitaker

    In this gripping, multi-layered thriller, Chris Whitaker weaves together a missing persons mystery, a serial killer tale, and a sweeping love story set against the backdrop of 1970s America. When a wealthy girl is targeted by a sinister force in the small town of Monta Clare, an unlikely hero emerges - Patch, a local boy who risks everything to save her. As Patch's search for the truth unfolds, Whitaker delves into the shadowy worlds of obsession, trauma, and the resilience of the human spirit. A riveting page-turner with a lyrical touch, All the Colors of the Dark is an absolutely must-read from a masterful storyteller.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. American Fantasy A Novel

    Emma Straub

    Join Annie on a delightful journey of self-discovery as she boards the American Fantasy cruise ship, where '90s heartthrobs reign supreme. A lighthearted and nostalgic tale, this novel reignites passion and possibility in middle age, as Annie reconnects with her youth and finds new meaning in unexpected places. A witty exploration of fame, aging, and love, Emma Straub's American Fantasy is the perfect escape.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. All About Love: The Deluxe Collector's Edition New Visions

    bell hooks

    "bell hooks brings her characteristic depth and passion to this groundbreaking exploration of love, revealing the devastating societal costs of lovelessness and offering a visionary new path to heal divisions and nurture compassion. With her incisive analysis and lyrical prose, hooks argues that love must be a verb, a proactive practice, to transform our world. A must-read for anyone seeking a more caring, united society and a deeper understanding of what it truly means to love."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

Frequently asked questions

What if I prefer graphic novels?
Heartstopper #6: A Graphic Novel by Alice Oseman offers a similar coming-of-age tone and tender relationships.
Are there any more whimsical suggestions?
The Phantom Tollbooth by Norton Juster features a magical, imaginative world and clever wordplay, echoing More Everything Forever's playful spirit.
Can you recommend a recent release?
Every Summer After by Carley Fortune is a contemporary romance with a nostalgic, sentimental quality reminiscent of More Everything Forever.
Are there any non-fiction options?
All About Love: The Deluxe Collector's Edition by bell hooks explores the complexities of love in a thoughtful, introspective manner
