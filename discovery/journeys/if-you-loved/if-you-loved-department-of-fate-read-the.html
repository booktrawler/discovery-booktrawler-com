If You Loved Department of Fate, Read These 6 Books

For fans of interdimensional intrigue and complex characters, try these thought-provoking novels with a touch of the surreal.

Who these books are for

  • Science fiction and fantasy enthusiasts
  • Readers who enjoy complex characters and plots
  • Fans of philosophical and metaphysical themes

Start here

If you only read one, choose Dept. of Speculation for its lyrical prose and poignant exploration of relationships.

Department of Fate's intricate world-building and character-driven story left you craving more? Delve into these six novels that share its captivating blend of science fiction, mystery, and philosophical depth. From interdimensional adventures to poignant explorations of the human experience, these read-alikes will keep you enthralled and pondering long after the final page.

The collection

  1. Dept. of Speculation

    Jenny Offill

    In 'Dept. of Speculation', Jenny Offill masterfully weaves together a mesmerizing tapestry of a modern marriage's rise and collapse. With prose as sleek and precise as it is emotionally charged, Offill crafts an intimate, unconventional novel that will resonate with anyone who's grappled with love, family, identity, and the search for meaning in a chaotic world. A triumph of concise, powerful storytelling, this book is a must-read for anyone seeking a profoundly affecting and deeply intelligent literary experience.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. The Ministry of Time A Novel

    Kaliane Bradley

    A dazzling, genre-bending debut, The Ministry of Time weaves a thrilling tale of time travel, espionage, and romance. When a modern civil servant is sent to monitor an 1845 Arctic expedition commander who's inexplicably appeared in the present, an unlikely bond forms and evolves into a profound love story. As secrets unfold, the protagonist must grapple with the consequences of her choices and whether she can alter the course of history. With wit, sensuality, and a captivating blend of science fiction and literary fiction, this original masterpiece by Kaliane Bradley will captivate readers and leave them pondering the power of love and change.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. A Court of Mist and Fury

    Sarah J. Maas

    Embrace the captivating world of Prythian as Feyre, the captive princess, awakens to the seductive allure of the Night Court. In this darkly enchanting follow-up to 'A Court of Thorns and Roses,' Sarah J. Maas masterfully weaves a tale of power, desire, and the unbreakable bonds of love. As Feyre navigates the treacherous politics of the fey realms and confronts the shadows within, she must choose between the man she once loved and the one who sets her soul on fire, forging a new destiny that will shake the very foundations of her world.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. The Department of Revenge How Trump Took Control of American Justice

    Devlin Barrett

    In this explosive exposé, Pulitzer Prize-winning journalist Devlin Barrett uncovers the systematic dismantling of the Justice Department during Trump's presidency, revealing how the administration weaponized the department for personal revenge against political enemies and critics. From the daily guidance of Trump advisor Stephen Miller to the "charge first, ask questions later" approach used against disliked targets, this gripping book offers a chilling inside look at the damage done and a roadmap for restoring the integrity of America's justice system.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. The Book of Dust: La Belle Sauvage (Book of Dust, Volume 1)

    Philip Pullman

    Step into the enchanting world of Philip Pullman's latest masterpiece, The Book of Dust: La Belle Sauvage, a stunning prequel to the beloved His Dark Materials trilogy. Follow Malcolm Polstead, an astute young boy, as he unravels the mysteries surrounding the enigmatic Lyra Belacqua, a girl destined for greatness. With its richly detailed setting, unforgettable characters, and Pullman's signature blend of fantasy and philosophical depth, this first volume of the Book of Dust trilogy is an unmissable adventure that will captivate readers of all ages.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. The Gate of the Feral Gods

    Matt Dinniman

    Join Carl and his unlikely team on an unpredictable adventure as they navigate the ever-shifting fifth floor of the galaxy's most thrilling game show. With new allies and fresh challenges around every corner, this hilarious and action-packed novel will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very end. Bonus material in this print edition adds even more excitement to this New York Times Bestseller.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. The Other Wes Moore One Name, Two Fates

    Wes Moore

    "Experience two parallel yet divergent paths in this gripping true tale of two young men, both named Wes Moore, born in the same Baltimore neighborhood yet destined for vastly contrasting fates - Rhodes Scholar and decorated veteran vs. life in prison. A poignant exploration of the choices that shape lives, this New York Times bestseller is a powerful, thought-provoking reflection on the American Dream and the resilience of the human spirit."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. A Court of Wings and Ruin

    Sarah J. Maas

    Diving into 'A Court of Wings and Ruin' is an immersive experience that will have you breathless and emotionally invested from the start. Sarah J. Maas masterfully weaves a lush, enchanting world with complex characters whose triumphs and heartaches resonate deeply. The romance is slow-burn perfection, the action is pulse-pounding, and the revelations will leave you reeling. Be prepared to lose yourself completely in this captivating tale of love, loyalty, and the unbreakable bonds of family.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. Iron Flame

    Rebecca Yarros

    "Immerse yourself in the epic romance of 'Iron Flame', where passion ignites amidst the backdrop of war-torn Afghanistan. Rebecca Yarros masterfully weaves a tale of forbidden love, heart-wrenching loss, and unrelenting resilience, evoking a visceral emotional response with every turn of the page. Get ready to succumb to the fiery intensity of this unforgettable story that will leave you breathless and yearning for more."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. The Ministry for the Future

    Kim Stanley Robinson

    Dive into the vivid, deeply researched world of 'The Ministry for the Future' and let Kim Stanley Robinson's masterful storytelling whisk you away to a future that's both terrifying and tantalizing. Through the eyes of hardened climate refugees and visionary policymakers, you'll feel the weight of our world's ecological crises grinding against the relentless human drive to adapt, innovate, and thrive. This epic novel will leave you awestruck by the power of the human spirit to reimagine, rebuild, and resist - even in the darkest of times.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

Frequently asked questions

What genre are these read-alikes in?
These books span science fiction, fantasy, and literary fiction, all with elements of the surreal and philosophical.
Are these books as long and dense as Department of Fate?
While some are similarly complex, most of these novels are more compact, ranging from 250 to 400 pages, making them easier to digest.
Do these books have similar themes to Department of Fate?
Yes, common themes include the nature of reality, the consequences of choice, and the human condition, often explored through unique narrative structures.
