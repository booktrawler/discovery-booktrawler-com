Where to Start with One Dark Window

Begin with One Dark Window, a dark fantasy novel that masterfully blends eerie atmosphere and intricate plotting.

Who these books are for

  • Fans of dark fantasy and horror seeking a new favorite series
  • Readers who appreciate atmospheric, suspenseful storytelling
  • Literary enthusiasts looking for complex, imaginative world-building

Start here

If you only read one, choose One Dark Window for its hauntingly beautiful prose and intricate, layered narrative.

Immerse yourself in the haunting beauty of Rachel Gillig's debut novel, One Dark Window. This dark fantasy tale weaves a complex web of suspense, moral ambiguity, and eerie atmospherics, inviting readers to step into the shadowy world of the living and the dead. With its intricate plotting, nuanced characters, and evocative prose, One Dark Window is an unmissable entry point for fans of the genre.

The collection

  1. One Dark Window (Standard Hardcover Edition)

    Rachel Gillig

    Experience the eerie, magical world of One Dark Window, where destiny, deception, and forbidden love collide. Meet Elspeth, a maiden with a monster trapped in her mind, the Nightmare, whose protection comes at a sinister cost. Join her perilous quest with the treacherous Captain to save their kingdom from darkness before an ancient evil consumes her completely. Gems of Uprooted and For the Wolf, this dark gothic fantasy will captivate fans of ethereal, pulse-pounding tales.

  2. Escape Me Deluxe Limited Edition

    Tahereh Mafi

    In the explosive third installment of the Shatter Me series, the stakes are higher than ever. James, tired of being underestimated, plots his own destiny amidst the looming threats. Meanwhile, Rosabelle must control her feelings for James while staying true to the plan to destroy the rebel regime. With gut-wrenching revelations, blistering romance, and non-stop action, Escape Me is a heart-pounding ride through a world where thoughts and emotions are the ultimate opponents. This deluxe edition is a stunning addition to the Shatter Me universe, with a shimmering jacket, foil case, and holographic edges.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. Polostan Volume One of Bomb Light

    Neal Stephenson

    Dive into this sweeping historical epic, as Neal Stephenson masterfully weaves fact and fiction to recapture the tumultuous early 20th century. Follow Dawn Rae Bjornberg, a free-spirited heroine born to a cowboy clan in the American West, as her journey takes her from revolutionary Russia to the streets of Depression-era D.C. and finally to the burgeoning world of espionage in Soviet Russia. With rich historical detail and inventive plotting, Polostan is a thrilling start to the Bomb Light series that will captivate fans of historical fiction and espionage alike.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. Light From Uncommon Stars

    Ryka Aoki

    Embark on a defiantly joyful adventure through California's San Gabriel Valley with Light From Uncommon Stars, where cursed violins, Faustian bargains, and queer alien courtship unfold amidst fresh-made donuts. Shizuka Satomi pursues her final soul to lift a curse, but finds an unexpected love in Lan Tran, a retired starship captain turned single mom. As their lives intertwine, a story of magic, identity, and hope emerges, revealing a family worth crossing the universe for.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. The Book of Dust: La Belle Sauvage (Book of Dust, Volume 1)

    Philip Pullman

    Step into the enchanting world of Philip Pullman's latest masterpiece, The Book of Dust: La Belle Sauvage, a stunning prequel to the beloved His Dark Materials trilogy. Follow Malcolm Polstead, an astute young boy, as he unravels the mysteries surrounding the enigmatic Lyra Belacqua, a girl destined for greatness. With its richly detailed setting, unforgettable characters, and Pullman's signature blend of fantasy and philosophical depth, this first volume of the Book of Dust trilogy is an unmissable adventure that will captivate readers of all ages.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. How to Read a Book

    Monica Wood

    In this profound and uplifting novel, three individuals - Violet a young woman released from prison, Harriet a retired English teacher, and Frank a widower - find unexpected common ground at a Portland bookstore. As their lives intertwine, Monica Wood explores themes of guilt, second chances, and the transformative power of stories. Through honest, deeply humane portrayals, Wood reminds us that kindness, forgiveness, and the love of books can illuminate even the darkest paths to redemption and a life worth living.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. 100 Cupboards The Graphic Novel

    N. D. Wilson

    Dive into the thrilling adventures within the mysterious cupboards of Henry's bedroom wall. When Henry discovers 100 tiny doors leading to unknown worlds, he's drawn into a fantastical quest to rescue his brave cousin Henrietta and prevent sinister forces from invading their own reality. With vivid graphics and an imaginative premise, this graphic novel adaptation of N. D. Wilson's bestselling series is a must-read for every young fantasy fan.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. The Shining

    Stephen King

    This chilling classic by master of horror Stephen King weaves a tale of psychological terror, supernatural dread, and the darkness lurking within family bonds. When Jack Torrance takes a job as caretaker of the isolated Overlook Hotel, he and his wife Wendy bring their son Danny, a child with an unsettling sixth sense. As the tormented father's sanity unravels and the hotel's evil forces intensify, King crafts a gripping, suspenseful story that will leave readers on the edge of their seats, desperate to uncover the hotel's sinister secrets before it's too late.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. The Everlasting

    Alix E. Harrow

    Dive into this enchanting tale of legend and love as historian Owen Mallory travels back in time to reenvision the story of Sir Una Everlasting, the orphaned heroine who became her nation's greatest hero. Their intertwined quest to rewrite history takes them on a fantastical journey, blending past and present in a captivating narrative that will keep you enthralled until the very end. With its flawless prose, rich world-building, and poignant exploration of storytelling, The Everlasting is a must-read for fans of fantasy and historical fiction.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. Field Guide for the Formerly Villainous (Deluxe Edition)

    Autumn K England

    Escape to Mossley's Rest, where a wounded former villain finds solace in simple living, budding friendships, and a forbidden romance. Autumn England's whimsical fantasy, starring a relatable and endearing protagonist Oaklin, crafts a heartwarming tale of healing and redemption amidst the enchanting backdrop of a magical farm. Rich with humor, charm, and a touch of darkness, this delightful read, reminiscent of Stardew Valley and Studio Ghibli, is perfect for cozy fantasy fans seeking a feel-good story.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

Frequently asked questions

Is One Dark Window part of a series?
Yes, it's the first book in the 'Dark Window' series by Rachel Gillig.
What themes does the novel explore?
One Dark Window delves into the shadows of human nature, exploring themes of morality, power, and the blurred lines between good and evil.
What's the typical reading time for the standard hardcover edition?
The average reading time for One Dark Window is around 10-12 hours, depending on your reading speed.
