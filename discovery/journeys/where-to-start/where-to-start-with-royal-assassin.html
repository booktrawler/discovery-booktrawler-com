Where to Start with Royal Assassin

Begin with Assassin's Apprentice, the first book in Robin Hobb's acclaimed Farseer Trilogy, before diving into Royal Assassin and Assassin's Quest for a complete, immersive experience.

Who these books are for

  • New readers of the Farseer Trilogy
  • Fans of Robin Hobb's epic fantasy
  • Viewers looking for a series to binge-read
  • Book clubs exploring fantasy literature

Start here

If you only read one, choose Assassin's Apprentice to launch your captivating journey through the world of Fitz and the Farseer Court.

Embark on a thrilling adventure with the Royal Assassin series by Robin Hobb, starting with the acclaimed Farseer Trilogy. Assassin's Apprentice sets the stage for a captivating journey through the world of fantasy, introducing readers to the gripping story of Fitz, a young assassin-in-training. Dive into this epic tale of politics, magic, and coming-of-age, and discover why the Farseer Trilogy is a beloved classic in the fantasy genre.

The collection

  1. Royal Assassin

    Robin Hobb

    Immerse yourself in the gritty, spellbinding world of Fitz, the reluctant royal assassin, as he navigates treachery, sacrifice, and the darker side of court politics in Robin Hobb's enthralling fantasy, Royal Assassin. Praised by George R.R. Martin and Locus magazine, this epic tale of survival and redemption will keep you on the edge of your seat.

  2. Warrior Princess Assassin A Novel

    Brigid Kemmerer

    In Brigid Kemmerer's addictive adult fantasy debut, interwoven fates and forbidden passions ignite amidst political intrigue and ancient magic. Meet Princess Jory, duty-bound to secure her kingdom's survival through a marriage of convenience with feared warrior King Maddox Kyronan. But their budding desires are threatened by Jory's secret: her family's magic is fading. Enter Asher, a former nobleman turned deadly assassin, whose job to kill Jory and Ky sets him on a collision course with his own heart. Will this trio navigate treacherous alliances, hidden deceptions, and their own increasingly irresistible attractions to save their realms and discover the true cost of love? With pulse-pounding action, lush world-building, and smoldering romance, Warrior Princess Assassin is your next obsession

  3. Assassin's Quest

    Robin Hobb

    In the riveting final installment of Robin Hobb's Farseer Trilogy, Fitz, believed dead but living on in spirit, seeks bloody retribution against his treacherous half-brother Regal for the murder of their father, King Shrewd. This dark and mesmerizing tale of politics, power, and personal struggle will draw you in, as Fitz's epic journey tests the very limits of his Farseer heritage and magic. A must-read for any fan of fantasy and unforgettable characters in a richly crafted world.

Frequently asked questions

Is Royal Assassin a standalone novel?
No, Royal Assassin is the second book in the Farseer Trilogy by Robin Hobb.
What genre is the Royal Assassin series?
Epic fantasy with elements of historical fiction, with complex world-building and character development.
What age range is suitable for the Farseer Trilogy?
Due to mature themes and violence, the series is recommended for readers 16 and above.
