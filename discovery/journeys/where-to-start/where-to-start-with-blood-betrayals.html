Where to Start with Blood & Betrayals

For a captivating introduction, begin with Jeanette Rose's Blood & Betrayals, then continue with Britney S. Lewis's Blood Lust for a well-rounded taste of the genre.

Who these books are for

  • Fans of dark fantasy and paranormal romance seeking a compelling series
  • Readers new to the genre looking for a engaging starting point

Start here

If you only read one, choose Blood & Betrayals by Jeanette Rose to immerse yourself in the captivating world of murder, magic, and mystery.

The Blood & Betrayals genre, characterized by dark fantasy, paranormal elements, and often a crime or mystery plot, offers a captivating blend of intrigue and supernatural thrills. For newcomers, navigating this rich and varied landscape can be daunting. This guide provides a strategic entry point and a suggested progression to help you dive into the genre and discover your new favorite series.

The collection

With a lush, atmospheric setting and a cast of complex characters, this instant USA Today bestseller will enthrall fans of Zodiac Academy and The Atlas Six series. Frequently asked questions

What makes Blood & Betrayals a great starting point?
It seamlessly blends elements of fantasy, romance, and crime fiction, providing an immersive and engaging introduction to the genre.
Can I follow up with more light-hearted fantasy after Blood & Betrayals?
Absolutely, as the genre offers a wide range of tones and styles. Travis Baldree's Legends & Lattes is a great example of a humorous urban fantasy that provides a nice contrast.
Will reading Blood & Betrayals spoil other books in the genre?
No, the story stands alone and doesn't heavily reference other works. The rich world-building and unique magic system make it a distinctive entry in the blood & betrayal sub-genre.
