Where to Start with The Polysecure Workbook

Begin with The Polysecure Workbook by Jessica Fern for a comprehensive, structured journey into polysecure attachment and non-monogamy.

Who these books are for

  • Individuals curious about polysecure attachment and non-monogamy
  • Couples exploring polyamory or open relationships
  • Therapists seeking to support clients navigating complex attachments

Start here

If you only read one, choose The Polysecure Workbook - it's the definitive resource on this topic.

The Polysecure Workbook represents a groundbreaking approach to understanding and cultivating healthy, secure attachments within non-monogamous relationships. By applying the principles of polysecure attachment to real-life scenarios, readers can develop a deeper sense of emotional safety, trust, and intimacy with their partners. This comprehensive guide demystifies polyamory and open relationships, empowering individuals to build strong, fulfilling connections.

Frequently asked questions

Is The Polysecure Workbook only for polyamorous individuals?
No, while it's rooted in polyamory, the principles can apply to anyone seeking to understand and secure their attachments.
Does The Polysecure Workbook require prior knowledge of attachment theory?
No, it's designed as an accessible, standalone guide that introduces key concepts and tools for understanding and working with polysecure attachment.
Can I use The Polysecure Workbook in a therapeutic setting?
Yes, it provides a framework for therapists to understand and support clients navigating complex attachments and non-monogamous relationships.
