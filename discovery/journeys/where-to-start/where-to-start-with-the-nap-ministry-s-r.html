Where to Start with The Nap Ministry's Rest Deck

For a foundational understanding of rest as radical self-care and resistance, begin with Tricia Hersey's 'The Nap Ministry's Rest Deck'. This deck offers practical tools to help you prioritize rest and recognize its power in social justice movements.

Who these books are for

  • Anyone seeking a deeper understanding of rest as a radical act of self-care and social justice
  • Those looking to integrate rest into their personal and professional lives
  • Individuals interested in exploring rest as a tool for resilience and activism

Start here

If you only read one, choose 'The Nap Ministry's Rest Deck' for the most comprehensive introduction to rest as resistance.

In today's fast-paced world, rest often takes a backseat to productivity and progress. However, rest is not laziness or avoidance, but rather a powerful form of resistance and radical self-care. Tricia Hersey, founder of The Nap Ministry, has been a leading voice in championing rest as a tool for social justice. Her work encourages individuals to reclaim rest as a necessary act of self-love and defiance against systems of oppression. With 'The Nap Ministry's Rest Deck', readers can begin to understand rest not just as a personal need, but as a political act with far-reaching implications for their own lives and for the world around them.

Frequently asked questions

What is The Nap Ministry's Rest Deck about?
This deck provides a framework for understanding rest as a form of resistance and radical self-care, offering practical tools and exercises to prioritize rest in daily life.
Is The Nap Ministry's Rest Deck only for activists or protesters?
No, while rest is a powerful tool for social justice movements, this deck is designed for anyone seeking to integrate rest into their lives as a means of self-care and resilience.
Can I use The Nap Ministry's Rest Deck even if I'm not a fan of napping?
Yes, while napping is a key component, the deck encompasses a broader understanding of rest that includes periods of relaxation, meditation, and quiet time – so there's something for everyone
