Where to Start with The Defining Decade

Begin with Meg Jay's The Defining Decade to gain a foundational understanding of the critical decade of your twenties and how to make intentional choices for your life's trajectory.

Who these books are for

  • Young adults seeking life direction
  • Twenty-somethings looking to make meaningful choices
  • Parents of emerging adults wanting to understand their child's journey

Start here

If you only read one, choose The Defining Decade to grasp the significance of your twenties and how to optimize this pivotal period.

Meg Jay's The Defining Decade revolutionized the way we think about the twenties by highlighting their profound impact on our long-term lives. In this landmark work of personal development, Jay presents a compelling case for why this decade matters most and offers practical strategies for harnessing its transformative power. By illuminating the psychological and social forces at play, The Defining Decade empowers readers to make intentional choices that will set the course for their future success and happiness.

Frequently asked questions

What is The Defining Decade about?
Meg Jay's book explores the psychological and social impacts of your twenties on long-term life outcomes, emphasizing the importance of intentional decision-making during this decade.
Who is The Defining Decade for?
The book is geared toward individuals in their twenties, but also provides valuable insights for parents, educators, and anyone interested in the social and emotional development of young adults.
What are some key takeaways from The Defining Decade?
Jay emphasizes the potential for rapid personal growth, encourages young people to seek fulfilling careers, and stresses the importance of building strong, supportive relationships during this formative decade.
