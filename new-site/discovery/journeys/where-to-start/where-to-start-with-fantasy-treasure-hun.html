Where to Start with Fantasy Treasure Hunts

For a captivating introduction to fantasy treasure hunts, begin with Katherine Rundell's Vanishing Treasures, a thrilling tale of adventure and discovery set in the Amazon rainforest.

Who these books are for

  • Fantasy readers seeking adventure and treasure hunts
  • New readers of the genre looking for an engaging starting point
  • Parents and educators seeking books for children and young adults

Start here

If you only read one, choose Vanishing Treasures for its rich world-building, memorable characters, and pulse-pounding treasure hunt.

Fantasy treasure hunts offer an irresistible combination of adventure, mystery, and reward for intrepid readers. These captivating stories transport us to enchanted realms, ancient ruins, and hidden temples in pursuit of long-lost treasures and powerful artifacts. Whether you're new to the fantasy genre or a seasoned fan, embarking on a treasure hunt narrative can be a thrilling and immersive experience.

The collection

  1. Vanishing Treasures A Bestiary of Extraordinary Endangered Creatures

    Katherine Rundell

    "Join acclaimed author Katherine Rundell on a spellbinding journey to encounter 23 extraordinary creatures on the brink of extinction. From the choreographed romance of seahorses to the miraculous resilience of frozen wood frogs and the playful, social lemurs, Rundell brings these remarkable animals to vivid life. With humor, passion, and stunning illustrations, Vanishing Treasures is a poignant tribute to nature's wonders - and a powerful call to action to preserve them for future generations."

  2. Annihilation A Novel (10th Anniversary Edition)

    Jeff VanderMeer

    Dive into the unsettling world of Area X, a realm where nature has consumed the remnants of human civilization, in Jeff VanderMeer's gripping debut novel, Annihilation. Join a team of four women, each with their own motivations and secrets, as they venture into the unknown, confronting lifeforms that defy explanation and unravel the mysteries that lurk within themselves. This critically-acclaimed, Nebula Award-winning tale weaves together elements of science fiction, horror, and literary fiction to create a thought-provoking and deeply unsettling journey that will leave readers questioning the very boundaries of reality.

  3. Verity Vox and the Curse of Foxfire

    Don Martin

    In the hauntingly atmospheric town of Foxfire, young witch-in-training Verity Vox must confront her magical limits to unravel a decades-old curse and rescue a missing girl. Blending Appalachian folklore with supernatural thrills, this gripping tale delves into the shadows of a forgotten coal mining town where secrets dwell beneath the ancient hills and the line between magic and madness blurs. Pick up Verity Vox and the Curse of Foxfire for a spellbinding journey into the heart of darkness and the resilience of the human spirit.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. Percy Jackson and the Olympians, Book One: The Lightning Thief

    Rick Riordan

    Embark on a thrilling quest with Percy Jackson, a troubled teenager who suddenly discovers he's the son of Poseidon, Greek god of the sea. When Zeus's stolen lightning bolt points to Percy as the prime suspect, he must team up with a satyr and demigod daughter of Athena to clear his name and save the realm of Olympus from war. Filled with action, humor, and fantastical creatures from Greek mythology brought to life, this captivating first book in the Percy Jackson series is an unforgettable adventure perfect for readers of all ages.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. Bookshops & Bonedust

    Travis Baldree

    Join Viv, a fierce orc mercenary, as she steps away from the battlefield to heal in the unassuming beach town of Murk. When not recovering from her injury, she finds herself drawn to a local bookshop, where a new sort of adventure awaits. With its unlikely protagonist, charming setting, and whimsical plot, Bookshops & Bonedust is a captivating tale of first loves, second chances, and the magic found in the most unexpected of places.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. The Fragile Threads of Power

    V. E. Schwab

    In this electrifying new adventure from V.E. Schwab, magic and danger collide across three Londons, threatening to unravel the fragile balance of power. Renegade thief Delilah Bard, child queen Kosika, and mysterious Tes must navigate treacherous alliances, ancient prophecies, and the dark allure of each world before they succumb to the very forces that seek to destroy them. With old friends and foes alike at risk, Schwab masterfully weaves a thrilling tale of magic, intrigue, and the unbreakable bonds that forge heroes and legends.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. Impossible Creatures

    Katherine Rundell

    Embark on a thrilling quest with Christopher and Mal as they adventure across the Archipelago, a magical realm teeming with mythical creatures. Racing against extinction, the unlikely duo must discover the cause of the waning magic and save the fantastical world before it's too late. Packed with over 60 illustrations, Katherine Rundell's "Impossible Creatures" is an electrifying journey that will captivate readers of all ages with its vivid imagination and heartfelt heroism.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. Artifacts

    Natalie Lemle

    Dive into Natalie Lemle's gripping debut, Artifacts, as successful attorney Lena Connolly is drawn into a mystery that resurrects her troubled past on an Italian archaeological dig. As she uncovers secrets surrounding a rare, looted artifact, Lena must confront the truth about her mentors' disappearances and the shadowy forces that still threaten her today. Alternating between past and present, this thrilling literary page-turner exposes the underworld of stolen relics, revealing the intricate networks between museums, crime, and the desperation to preserve our cultural heritage.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. Cloud Atlas A Novel

    David Mitchell

    Embark on a mesmerizing journey through time and space with David Mitchell's breathtaking Cloud Atlas, a genre-bending masterpiece that weaves six interconnected stories spanning from the 19th century to a postapocalyptic future. Witness the transformative power of human connections as lives and destinies entwine across ages, in a tale of love, betrayal, and resilience that defies conventional narrative structures. This critically acclaimed, award-winning novel will transport and inspire you, cementing its place as a timeless classic of 21st-century literature.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. Twig's Traveling Tomes

    Gryffin Murphy

    "Embark on a mesmerizing journey through time and imagination with Twig's Traveling Tomes, Gryffin Murphy's enchanting literary odyssey. As our protagonist curates a whimsical collection of rare books, each volume transports us to a captivating realm - a mysterious forest, an opulent palace, and even a floating city. With lyrical prose and imaginative storytelling, Murphy weaves a tapestry of wonder that explores the power of knowledge, the allure of the unknown, and the magic found within the pages of a treasured tale. Get lost in the spell of Twig's Tomes, where the boundaries of reality blur and the possibilities are endless."

Frequently asked questions

Are treasure hunts a common element in fantasy novels?
Yes, treasure hunts and the pursuit of lost artifacts are recurring themes in fantasy literature, often driving plot and character development.
Can I start with books for older readers or children if I prefer?
Absolutely, the fantasy treasure hunt subgenre spans a range of age groups and reading levels. This guide provides recommendations suitable for various ages and tastes.
Will these books be similar in style and tone?
While all feature fantasy treasure hunts, the tone, style, and target audience vary between the selected novels. This diversity allows readers to find an entry point that best suits their preferences.
