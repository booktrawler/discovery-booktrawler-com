If You Loved 'A Marriage at Sea', Read These 9 Books

Discover more stories of love, adventure, and self-discovery with these captivating novels that share the emotional depth and nautical settings of 'A Marriage at Sea'.

Who these books are for

  • Fans of emotional, character-driven fiction
  • Readers who enjoy stories with a strong nautical or travel theme
  • Those seeking books that explore love, relationships, and self-discovery

Start here

If you only read one, choose 'Somewhere Beyond the Sea' by TJ Klune for its heartwarming love story and poignant exploration of identity.

If the sweeping romance, personal growth, and nautical adventure of 'A Marriage at Sea' have you hooked, you're likely eager to dive into more captivating tales that explore the human heart and the allure of the open sea. From heartwarming love stories to poignant explorations of identity and belonging, these 9 books offer the perfect blend of emotional depth and captivating settings to keep you enthralled.

The collection

  1. Somewhere Beyond the Sea

    TJ Klune

    Dive into a mesmerizing tale of forbidden love and second chances with TJ Klune's heart-stirring novel, Somewhere Beyond the Sea. When navigator Dani Ramos returns to his hometown years after a shattering summer, he's forced to confront the ocean of emotions he never thought he'd feel again - until he reconnects with the one person able to chart a course for his heart: Ryan, his first love and the reason he left in the first place. Klune masterfully weaves together themes of identity, family, and the power of embracing one's true self in this poignant, deeply romantic exploration of what it means to find home in both the places we're from and the people we love.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. Man Overboard! A Novel

    Kathleen Rooney

    Man Overboard! by Kathleen Rooney is an inventive, hilarious, and heartwarming summer read about a former college swimmer who plunges into the ocean and must cling to life while confronting his complicated family and painful memories. With its surprising twists and uplifting message, this novel will have readers laughing, crying, and cheering for the protagonist's survival against the odds, perfect for fans of Where'd You Go, Bernadette and Florence Adler Swims Forever.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. The Bookshop by the Bay A Novel

    Pamela M. Kelley

    In Pamela Kelley's heartwarming novel, two lifelong friends rediscover their passions and second chances in the charming coastal town of Cape Cod. As Jess, a high-profile lawyer, and Alison, a magazine editor, revive Alison's beloved bookstore, they nurse their own battered dreams and forge a new path forward alongside their complex family relationships. A delightful, multi-generational story about friendship, food, coffee, and the transformative power of a good book, The Bookshop by the Bay is perfect for anyone seeking a feel-good summer escape.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. In the Heart of the Sea The Tragedy of the Whaleship Essex (National Book Award Winner)

    Nathaniel Philbrick

    Dive into the epic true tale that inspired Herman Melville's Moby-Dick. In the Heart of the Sea chronicles the harrowing 1820 sinking of the whaleship Essex by a furious sperm whale, leaving its desperate crew adrift for over 90 days in tiny boats. This National Book Award-winning saga combines thrilling maritime adventure with deep historical context, offering a gripping narrative steeped in the lore of Nantucket whaling. A must-read for anyone captivated by survival against the odds.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. The Old Man and the Sea The Hemingway Library Edition

    Ernest Hemingway

    In this timeless, elegantly crafted tale, Hemingway masterfully weaves themes of resilience, courage, and tragic beauty around the epic struggle of an aging fisherman against a monstrous marlin. A supreme showcase of the author's distinctive prose style, The Old Man and the Sea is a 20th-century classic sure to captivate readers with its poignant, potent storytelling.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. Pool House A Novel

    Mary H.K. Choi

    In this bold, genre-defying debut, Mary H.K. Choi masterfully navigates the complexities of mother-daughter relationships, Hollywood, and the struggles of growing up and growing older. When Stevie flees her suffocating life with her eccentric, grieving actress mother Moon to the subterranean world of a dead-end job, they're forced into a cramped, glass-walled pool house in their own backyard. With the added presence of Adam, Moon's late TV son and Stevie's longtime crush, this razor-sharp exploration of class, fame, identity, and family deception simmers with wit, humor, and raw emotion, offering a refreshing, deeply relatable portrayal of women's lives in Los Angeles.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. The Covenant of Water (Oprah's Book Club)

    Abraham Verghese

    Dive into this unforgettable literary epic, The Covenant of Water, a sweeping tale of love, faith, and medicine spanning three generations of a Kerala, South India family. Despite their Christian heritage, a mysterious and tragic curse of drowning strikes each generation, yet they persevere through hardship and triumph. With Oprah's stamp of approval, this masterful novel from Abraham Verghese is a transportive and unputdownable journey that will leave you in awe of the human spirit.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. The Bookstore on the Beach

    Brenda Novak

    Picking up The Bookstore on the Beach, Brenda Novak's captivating novel, is like opening a treasured, weathered tome - you'll be immediately drawn in. Amidst a summer of secrets, heartache, and change in a quaint coastal town, Autumn must navigate the uncertain path to healing and rediscovering love. With its heartfelt storytelling and witty charm, this tale will resonate deeply as Autumn crafts a new chapter, one page at a time. Don't miss this moving romance that will leave you with a warm, satisfied glow.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. The Isle in the Silver Sea

    Tasha Suri

    In this captivating standalone fantasy romance, Tasha Suri weaves a centuries-old tale of forbidden love between a knight and a witch, doomed to fall and rise together in an ever-repeating cycle. Against the vibrant backdrop of medieval England, Simran and Vina must navigate their desires and risk it all to break the curse, as a mysterious threat targets their kind of fated stories. A sensuous, haunting exploration of love that transcends time, The Isle in the Silver Sea will keep you enthralled until its breathtaking conclusion.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. The Beach Reads Bookshop A Small Town Romance

    Lee Tobin McClain

    Step into the charming world of The Beach Reads Bookshop, where romance blossoms amidst shelves of beloved stories. Billionaire Luis Dominguez and bookstore manager Deena Clark navigate single fatherhood and second chances, their bond strengthened by shared love of literature. A heartwarming tale set on a picturesque Chesapeake Bay island, this small-town romance from bestseller Lee Tobin McClain is the perfect escape for anyone who's ever found solace in a good book.

    Buy on Bookshop.org
