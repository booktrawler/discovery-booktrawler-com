FAQ

What is The Winners Circle?

A series of 10 short, powerful books on resilience, purpose, and clarity. Each book is a focused meditation on a single idea. All are free PDF downloads.

How do I download the books?

Click any "Read More" button to visit a book's page, then click the "Download Free PDF" button. No email required.

Who wrote these books?

The Winners Circle is published by tom/rhodes. Each book is written with care and precision, designed to deliver maximum insight in minimum time.

How long does each book take to read?

Each book is 8,000–10,000 words, roughly 9-12 chapters. Most readers finish one in a single sitting.
