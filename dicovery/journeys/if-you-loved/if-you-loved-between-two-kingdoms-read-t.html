If You Loved Between Two Kingdoms, Read These 8 Books

Dive into more masterful storytelling with these fantastical and atmospheric tales that blend history, magic, and memorable characters.

Who these books are for

  • Fantasy readers seeking rich world-building
  • Historical fiction enthusiasts drawn to alternate realities
  • Fans of literary storytelling with complex characters

Start here

If you only read one, choose A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms by George R.R. Martin for its captivating medieval setting and compelling characters.

Minae Noth's poignant and imaginative memoir Between Two Kingdoms captured readers with its vivid storytelling and exploration of identity, family, and the power of words. If you're craving more books that weave together history, magic, and memorable characters, this curated list is for you. From epic fantasy series to atmospheric historical fiction, these 8 novels share elements of Between Two Kingdoms' unique blend of the real and the imagined, inviting you to lose yourself in richly detailed worlds and complex characters.

The collection

  1. A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms

    George R. R. Martin

    Step into the Seven Kingdoms nearly a century before the events of A Game of Thrones in this riveting prequel trio from George R.R. Martin. Meet Ser Duncan the Tall, a towering knight with a heart of gold, and his loyal diminutive squire Egg, as they navigate a world of royal intrigue, powerful foes, and outrageous exploits. Featuring over 160 captivating illustrations, A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms is a must-read that seamlessly weaves rich history and vivid characters, making it a thrilling companion to Martin's iconic A Song of Ice and Fire series.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. Behind Five Willows

    June Hur

    Step behind the ornate walls of ancient Korea and immerse yourself in a captivating tale of forbidden literature, blossoming romance, and societal expectations. Meet Haewon, a determined young woman risking her safety to transcribe illegal books, and Seojun, a nobleman secretly pouring his heart into writing fiction. When their paths cross amidst a courtship fraught with judgment and censorship, they must confront their own biases and discover the power of words and love. Described as a beautifully crafted homage to Jane Austen's Pride and Prejudice, Behind Five Willows is a must-read for fans of historical romance and courageous stories of self-expression.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. This Kingdom Will Not Kill Me

    Ilona Andrews

    Transport yourself to a gritty, magic-fueled world inspired by Ilona Andrews' signature blend of politics, romance, and high-stakes adventure. When Maggie materializes naked in a back alley, her only lifeline is her exhaustive knowledge of the fictional city of Kair Toren, drawn from years of obsessive reading. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of warlords, assassins, and mythical creatures, Maggie finds herself entwined in the fates of these living characters, all while racing to prevent a catastrophic war. If you devour epic fantasy and portal fantasies like Game of Thrones and Outlander, dive into the captivating world of This Kingdom Will Not Kill Me, where the lines between reality and fiction blur in the most thrilling ways.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. Another Country

    James Baldwin

    Immerse yourself in James Baldwin's vivid portrayal of interwoven passions spanning Greenwich Village, Harlem, and France. In this groundbreaking novel, the masks of identity are stripped away by the raw intensity of love and hate, revealing the elemental beauty and sublime complexity of the human experience. A must-read for anyone seeking a deeply moving and thought-provoking exploration of race, sex, politics, and art.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. Between Two Fires

    Christopher Buehlman

    Dive into a dark medieval fantasy with Between Two Fires, a chilling tale of angels, demons, and the apocalypse. Disgraced knight Thomas joins a young orphan girl on a perilous quest to confront evil and find redemption, navigating a grotesque landscape of risen dead, demonic forces, and soaring angelic battles for humanity's very soul. This beautifully crafted hardcover edition is a must-read for fans of horror and historical fiction.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. Other Worlds Than These A Talisman Novel

    Stephen King

    Embark on an unforgettable adventure with beloved protagonist Jack Sawyer as he navigates treacherous realms in the thrilling finale to Stephen King and Peter Straub's Talisman Trilogy. In Other Worlds Than These, Jack must confront a rampaging gang of infected teens and the monstrous forces of the Gullet before they destroy our world and all others. With stunning black-and-white illustrations by Gabriel Rodriguez, this spectacular and propulsive tale promises heart, humor, and a dazzling display of King's masterful storytelling. Perfect for both new readers and Constant Fans, Other Worlds Than These is a must-read epic that will transport you to unforgettable realms and leave you breathless.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. J.R.R. Tolkien 4-Book Boxed Set: The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings The Hobbit, The Fellowship of the Ring, The Two Towers, The Return of the King

    J.R.R. Tolkien

    Step into Middle-earth with this magnificent 4-volume set, featuring J.R.R. Tolkien's beloved classic, The Hobbit, and the epic high fantasy trilogy, The Lord of the Rings. Join Bilbo Baggins on a thrilling adventure as he discovers hidden depths of courage and wit, and follow the perilous quest of Frodo and his Fellowship as they battle to save their world from the dark lord Sauron's grasp. Immerse yourself in Tolkien's richly detailed world of myth and legend, where orcs, elves, dwarves, and hobbits collide in a timeless battle between good and darkness. This complete boxed set is a must-have for any fantasy fan.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. A Court of Mist and Fury

    Sarah J. Maas

    Embrace the captivating world of Prythian as Feyre, the captive princess, awakens to the seductive allure of the Night Court. In this darkly enchanting follow-up to 'A Court of Thorns and Roses,' Sarah J. Maas masterfully weaves a tale of power, desire, and the unbreakable bonds of love. As Feyre navigates the treacherous politics of the fey realms and confronts the shadows within, she must choose between the man she once loved and the one who sets her soul on fire, forging a new destiny that will shake the very foundations of her world.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. Crooked Kingdom A Sequel to Six of Crows

    Leigh Bardugo

    In this gripping sequel to Six of Crows, Kaz and his crew of lovable misfits face their greatest challenge yet. Double-crossed, weakened and hunted, they must outwit ruthless foes in the dark streets of Ketterdam to survive. With pulse-pounding action, vivid world-building, and a cast of complex characters you'll root for, Crooked Kingdom is the un-put-down-able conclusion to the Six of Crows duology that will leave you breathless. Don't miss this thrilling adventure from #1 New York Times bestselling author Leigh Bardugo.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. Six of Crows Boxed Set Six of Crows, Crooked Kingdom

    Leigh Bardugo

    Join Kaz, Inej, Nina, Matthias, Jesper, and Wylan - six unlikely heroes with dangerous skills - as they embark on a thrilling heist through the dark alleys and hidden dangers of the Grishaverse. With magic, myth, and mayhem abounding, this duo of bestselling novels by Leigh Bardugo will keep you on the edge of your seat from start to finish.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

Frequently asked questions

Why are these recommendations similar to Between Two Kingdoms?
These books share elements of complex characters, historical settings, and fantastical or magical elements that made Between Two Kingdoms so compelling.
Can I find all of these on audiobook?
Yes, most of these titles are available in audiobook format, perfect for a immersive listening experience.
Are these books part of a series?
Some, like the series by George R.R. Martin and Leigh Bardugo, are part of larger ongoing series, while others are standalone novels.
