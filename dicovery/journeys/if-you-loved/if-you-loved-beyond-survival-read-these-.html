If You Loved Beyond Survival, Read These 9 Books

For fans of Beyond Survival's gripping survival stories and unique blends of adventure and introspection, these nine books offer similarly compelling journeys of resilience and self-discovery.

Who these books are for

  • Fans of survival fiction
  • Adventure enthusiasts
  • Readers seeking books with strong character development

Start here

If you only read one, choose Somewhere Beyond the Sea by TJ Klune for its heart-wrenching survival tale and poignant exploration of the human spirit.

Beyond Survival's compelling blend of survival skills, gripping adventure, and poignant character development has resonated with many readers. If you connected with its unique storytelling, you're likely to find kindred spirits in these nine recommended books. From harrowing survival tales to thought-provoking explorations of the human condition, these novels offer a diverse yet cohesive set of reading experiences sure to captivate fans of the genre.

The collection

  1. Somewhere Beyond the Sea

    TJ Klune

    Dive into a mesmerizing tale of forbidden love and second chances with TJ Klune's heart-stirring novel, Somewhere Beyond the Sea. When navigator Dani Ramos returns to his hometown years after a shattering summer, he's forced to confront the ocean of emotions he never thought he'd feel again - until he reconnects with the one person able to chart a course for his heart: Ryan, his first love and the reason he left in the first place. Klune masterfully weaves together themes of identity, family, and the power of embracing one's true self in this poignant, deeply romantic exploration of what it means to find home in both the places we're from and the people we love.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. Behind Five Willows

    June Hur

    Step behind the ornate walls of ancient Korea and immerse yourself in a captivating tale of forbidden literature, blossoming romance, and societal expectations. Meet Haewon, a determined young woman risking her safety to transcribe illegal books, and Seojun, a nobleman secretly pouring his heart into writing fiction. When their paths cross amidst a courtship fraught with judgment and censorship, they must confront their own biases and discover the power of words and love. Described as a beautifully crafted homage to Jane Austen's Pride and Prejudice, Behind Five Willows is a must-read for fans of historical romance and courageous stories of self-expression.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. Unreasonable Women Three Stories of Violence, Imprisonment, and Extraordinary Survival

    Justine van der Leun

    "In this groundbreaking work of literary reportage, award-winning journalist Justine van der Leun shines a light on the shocking reality of women being criminalized for surviving abuse. Through the riveting stories of Tanisha, Jema, and TC - three unforgettable women pushed to extremes to protect themselves and their loved ones - van der Leun exposes the dark underbelly of a justice system that often punishes the very survivors it should be safeguarding. With intimate, propulsive narratives and unprecedented access to U.S. prisons, Unreasonable Women is a searing call to action and a testament to the resilience of the human spirit in the face of unimaginable trauma and adversity."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. Vanishing Treasures A Bestiary of Extraordinary Endangered Creatures

    Katherine Rundell

    "Join acclaimed author Katherine Rundell on a spellbinding journey to encounter 23 extraordinary creatures on the brink of extinction. From the choreographed romance of seahorses to the miraculous resilience of frozen wood frogs and the playful, social lemurs, Rundell brings these remarkable animals to vivid life. With humor, passion, and stunning illustrations, Vanishing Treasures is a poignant tribute to nature's wonders - and a powerful call to action to preserve them for future generations."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. Democracy or Else How to Save America in 10 Easy Steps

    Jon Favreau

    From the creators of Pod Save America comes this timely, illustrated guide to taking action and making a difference in American politics. With a mix of humor and practical advice, "Democracy or Else" breaks down the complex process into 10 manageable steps for getting informed, donating, volunteering, organizing, and more. Featuring insights from savvy experts and refreshingly down-to-earth politicians, this book is the perfect resource for political newcomers and seasoned activists alike, empowering readers to join the fight to save democracy in the face of unprecedented challenges and contradictions.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. Hell Followed with Us

    Andrew Joseph White

    "Join Benji, a fierce trans teenager, in his fight against the apocalyptic cult that raised him and now seeks to exploit his mutated powers. Finding refuge with a group of LGBTQ+ teens at the Acheson Center, Benji must learn to control his deadly new abilities while uncovering the leader's secrets. A thrilling, queer debut filled with action, heart, and a defiant stance against the monsters of oppression."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. Dungeon Crawler Carl

    Matt Dinniman

    "Dungeon Crawler Carl" delves into the gritty world of RPG obsessives, where the line between fantasy and reality blurs. This humorous yet insightful novel follows Carl, a lovable everyman trapped in the endless loop of dungeon crawls, as he grapples with the consequences of his addiction and the friends who dare to intervene. With a keen eye for geek culture and a poignant exploration of loneliness, 'Dungeon Crawler Carl' is a relatable and entertaining romp through the uncharted realms of gaming fandom.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. The Other Pandemic An AIDS Memoir

    Lynn Curlee

    Step into the heart of the 1980s and 1990s AIDS epidemic with Lynn Curlee's searing, photo-illustrated memoir. A firsthand account of the devastating pandemic that ravaged the gay community, this powerful story of loss, resilience, and the struggle for rights still echoes today. As Curlee recounts the decline of friends and loved ones, including his own life partner, he reveals the parallels between this historical crisis and our current pandemic reality. A vital read for understanding the past and its powerful impact on the present, The Other Pandemic is a poignant tribute to those lost and a call to never forget the lessons of that turbulent era.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. It Lasts Forever and Then It's Over

    Anne de Marcken

    In Anne de Marcken's genre-bending novel It Lasts Forever and Then It's Over, a voraciously alive heroine navigates the bizarre landscapes of the afterlife, yearning for the love and humanity she left behind. With a dead but opinionated crow in her chest, she encounters a series of terrifying, hilarious, and heart-wrenching situations, shedding pieces of her body and self along the way. A bold and imaginative tale that probes mortality's transformative power, this acclaimed novel delivers a bracing and unforgettable reading experience.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. A Good Girl's Guide to Murder

    Holly Jackson

    In this gripping page-turner, Pip sets out to uncover the truth behind her friend's supposed murder, revealing a tangled web of secrets in her small town. With each new twist, Pip's own life is put in danger, propelling her toward an unexpected ending. Perfect for mystery lovers and fans of complex, suspenseful teen thrillers.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

Frequently asked questions

Are these books primarily about survival?
While survival is a key element, these books delve deeper into the characters' emotional journeys and the ways they grow through adversity.
Can I expect fast-paced action from these recommendations?
Yes, many feature intense, thrilling scenes, but the pacing and emphasis vary between books.
Are these adult or young adult novels?
The selection includes both adult and YA titles, so readers of various ages can find something that resonates with them.
