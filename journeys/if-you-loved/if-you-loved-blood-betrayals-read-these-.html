If You Loved Blood & Betrayals, Read These 9 Books

For fans of Blood & Betrayals' dark, suspenseful storytelling, these captivating page-turners offer a mix of mystery, romance, and fantasy to keep you on the edge of your seat.

Who these books are for

  • Fans of dark, suspenseful fiction
  • Readers of mystery, romance, and fantasy
  • Book clubs looking for thought-provoking discussions

Start here

If you only read one, choose A Good Girl's Guide to Murder for its clever whodunit plot and atmospheric setting.

Blood & Betrayals captivates readers with its intricate plot, morally ambiguous characters, and dark, suspenseful atmosphere. If you're craving more stories that explore the human condition through the lens of mystery, romance, and fantasy, these 9 captivating books offer a similar reading experience. From clever whodunits to fantastical tales of love and power, this curated list will transport you to new worlds and keep you guessing until the very end.

The collection

  1. Blood Lust

    Britney S. Lewis

    In this gripping sequel to the #1 NYT bestseller Blood Moon, Mira confronts a world of vampire royalty and deadly trials as she races to rescue her kidnapped parents. With trust brittle after Julian's betrayal, Mira must rely on the vampire she loves to guide her through the treacherous court and her own dark transformation. As the stakes grow higher, Mira's loyalties will be tested and her very humanity threatened in Blood Lust, the electrifying second book in The Blood Chronicles series.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. A Good Girl's Guide to Murder

    Holly Jackson

    In this gripping page-turner, Pip sets out to uncover the truth behind her friend's supposed murder, revealing a tangled web of secrets in her small town. With each new twist, Pip's own life is put in danger, propelling her toward an unexpected ending. Perfect for mystery lovers and fans of complex, suspenseful teen thrillers.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. Fire & Blood 300 Years Before A Game of Thrones

    George R. R. Martin

    Embark on a thrilling journey through the centuries-spanning history of House Targaryen, from Aegon the Conqueror to the brutal Dance of the Dragons. In this masterful chronicle, George R.R. Martin reveals the untold tales of the dragonlords, their struggles for power, and the dark secrets behind their enigmatic reign. With vivid descriptions of legendary battles, politics, and the enigmatic magic of the First Men, Fire & Blood is a must-read for any fan of A Song of Ice and Fire series and epic fantasy alike.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. Roots & Wrath

    E J Wenstrom

    "Prepare for a spellbinding thrill ride as five former friends-turned-witches are forced back together by a deadly pact. Nineteen years after their dark ritual in the woods, deceit and murder stalk them as they race to uncover the traitor in their midst. With a ghostly past hauntingly lurking, only the most ruthless can survive the treacherous path to break their magical bond - and the chance to escape their dark pasts once and for all."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. A Court of Thorns and Roses 6

    Sarah J. Maas

    Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of Prythian once more as Sarah J. Maas returns to the captivating courts with A Court of Thorns and Roses 6. This spellbinding new installment delivers all the romance, danger, and intrigue fans of the beloved series have come to love. Dive back in and discover what awaits Feyre and her comrades in the next thrilling chapter of their epic journey.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. The First Time I Saw Him

    Laura Dave

    In this captivating follow-up to The Last Thing He Told Me, Laura Dave masterfully weaves another spellbinding tale, this time delving into themes of forgiveness and the enduring power of love. Picking up where the previous story left off, The First Time I Saw Him promises an equally gripping, rich, and evocative reading experience, sure to leave readers eagerly turning the pages to unravel the next chapter in this unforgettable saga.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. Begin Again James Baldwin's America and Its Urgent Lessons for Our Own

    Eddie S. Glaude Jr.

    "In this powerful exploration, Eddie Glaude delves into James Baldwin's life and work to reveal critical lessons for our tumultuous era. Begin Again is a searing analysis of America's enduring struggle with racism, weaving together biography, history, and personal reflection. Glaude's insightful interpretation of Baldwin's experiences during the civil rights movement offers a vital roadmap for navigating our current moment of racial reckoning and political upheaval."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. Before I Let Go

    Kennedy Ryan

    Rediscover the love that once seemed destined to last forever in Kennedy Ryan's impassioned second chance romance, Before I Let Go. When Yasmen and Josiah Wade are drawn back together after a decade apart, their rekindled attraction ignites a deep longing to reclaim their relationship. But the scars of their painful past threaten to tear them asunder once more. A heart-wrenching, intensely sensual journey that probes the depths of love, forgiveness, and second chances, Before I Let Go is a triumph from a New York Times bestseller sure to leave readers breathless.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. Red, White & Royal Blue: The Private Correspondence

    Casey McQuiston

    Step back into the unforgettable romance of Alex Claremont-Diaz and Henry Fox-Montaigne, reimagined in a captivating epistolary format. Red, White & Royal Blue: The Private Correspondence weaves a charming narrative through handwritten letters spanning over two decades, revealing the witty banter, tender vulnerabilities, and intimate passions of this beloved couple and their circle. Dive into the private archives to uncover the heartfelt milestones and everyday moments that make their enduring love story a treasured delight.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. The Man Who Died Twice A Thursday Murder Club Mystery

    Richard Osman

    Laugh-out-loud fun and clever mystery abound in this second installment of the Thursday Murder Club series from Richard Osman. Join Elizabeth, Joyce, Ron, and Ibrahim as they once again become amateur sleuths, this time dealing with a diamond heist and a ruthless killer. With their quick wit, charm, and experience, will the septuagenarian quartet catch the culprit before it's too late? Dive into this unputdownable, delightful read for an unforgettable detective adventure filled with humor and heart.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

Frequently asked questions

Is Blood & Betrayals a romance novel?
While it contains romantic elements, the story's primary focus is on the dark, suspenseful mystery and its complex characters.
Are the recommended books similarly dark?
While some have intense themes, the list balances darkness with lighter, more hopeful tales for a diverse reading experience.
Can I find all of these books in paperback?
Yes, most popular titles are available in various formats, including paperback, e-book, and audiobook, from major online retailers and bookstores.
