If You Loved The Irresistible Urge to Fall for Your Enemy, Read These

For more captivating tales of forbidden love and rivalry, pick up titles like The Exquisite Torment of Loving Your Enemy, Read This When Things Fall Apart, and The First Time I Saw Him.

Who these books are for

  • Fans of contemporary romance with a dark edge
  • Readers who devour enemies-to-lovers and forbidden love stories
  • Anyone seeking complex, emotionally charged tales of rivalry and desire

Start here

If you only read one, choose The Exquisite Torment of Loving Your Enemy for its intense, emotional portrayal of a love-hate relationship.

The Irresistible Urge to Fall for Your Enemy leaves readers craving more of the thrill, tension, and passion that comes with forbidden love and rivalry. This curated list delivers just that, featuring novels with complex characters, intense relationships, and a similar dark, romantic edge.

The collection

  1. The Exquisite Torment of Loving Your Enemy

    Brigitte Knightley

    In this steamy, swoony sequel, forbidden love ignites between Osric, an assassin, and Aurienne, a healer, as the deadly Pox ravages their world. With every healing session, their immutable differences blur, drawing them perilously close. As they unravel dark secrets and face unspeakable threats, Osric and Aurienne must navigate the treacherous landscape between love and hate, their hearts hanging in the balance. A lush, witty, and achingly romantic fantasy that will keep you breathless until the very end.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. Read This When Things Fall Apart Letters to Activists in Crisis

    Kelly Hayes

    When the struggles of activism lead to heartbreak, burnout, and despair, seek solace in Kelly Hayes' compassionate letters. Drawing from personal experience and inspiring stories from fellow change-makers, Hayes offers a lifeline for those grappling with the dark side of justice work. Part survival guide, part healing balm, these powerful letters remind us that even in the bleakest moments, we can find strength, hope, and the wisdom to keep fighting.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. The Poisoned King

    Katherine Rundell

    "Embark on a daring adventure in The Poisoned King, the enthralling sequel to Impossible Creatures. When a rare dragon summons Christopher Forrester to mythic Archipelago, he finds a world plagued by poison. With the help of fierce heroine Anya and an unforgettable cast of magical creatures, plunge into a perilous quest for justice - featuring 50 stunning illustrations that bring the fantastical to life. A thrilling must-read for young and old alike!"

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. The First Time I Saw Him

    Laura Dave

    In this captivating follow-up to The Last Thing He Told Me, Laura Dave masterfully weaves another spellbinding tale, this time delving into themes of forgiveness and the enduring power of love. Picking up where the previous story left off, The First Time I Saw Him promises an equally gripping, rich, and evocative reading experience, sure to leave readers eagerly turning the pages to unravel the next chapter in this unforgettable saga.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. Iron Flame (Wing and Claw Collection)

    Rebecca Yarros

    Step into a world of grit and magic in Rebecca Yarros' heart-pounding Empyrean series. Iron Flame begins where dryad Violet Sorrengail thought her story ended - surviving the deadly first year at Basgiath War College. But this is just the start of her tale, as she faces a new, merciless vice commandant and must choose between betraying her loved ones or forging her own path in a world where dragon riders rule by their own brutal laws. Dive into this stunning, #1 New York Times bestseller - perfect for fans of dark fantasy and fierce heroines - and discover the secrets hidden for centuries in this unforgettable series.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. The Ballad of Falling Dragons

    Sarah A Parker

    Immerse yourself in the breathtaking world of The Ballad of Falling Dragons, where ancient magic, mysterious creatures, and an unforgettable love story collide. This New York Times #1 bestseller and eagerly anticipated sequel to When the Moon Hatched promises a thrilling adventure as Raeve navigates vengeance, forbidden romance, and the fate of realms. With daring quests, burning secrets, and the promise of a world-changing revelation, this epic fantasy romance will captivate and leave you yearning for the next moonfall. Pick up this instant classic to be swept into a mesmerizing tale of passion, power, and self-discovery.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. Angel Down (Pulitzer Prize Winner) A Novel

    Daniel Kraus

    Diving into Daniel Kraus's Pulitzer Prize-winning novel 'Angel Down' is a visceral, gut-wrenching journey that will leave you emotionally battered yet strangely hopeful. Kraus masterfully crafts a haunting tale of a teenage girl's harrowing descent into the dark underbelly of a small Midwestern town, where the lines between innocence and depravity blur. As the protagonist's desperation builds towards a shattering climax, every page crackles with tension and raw, unflinching truths. By the end, you'll emerge drained but profoundly moved, forced to confront the darkest corners of the human psyche alongside this unflinching heroine.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. You Can Change the World

    Brad Meltzer

    Inspirational quotes and vibrant artwork from the acclaimed Ordinary People Change the World series come together in this uplifting graduation gift, featuring the courageous wisdom of trailblazers like Amelia Earhart, Albert Einstein, and Martin Luther King Jr. Perfect for heroes-in-training, it's a fun, friendly book to cherish and share.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. Lessons from Cats for Surviving Fascism

    Stewart Reynolds

    Purr-fectly timed for turbulent times, Lessons from Cats for Surviving Fascism distills feline wisdom into 11 subversive strategies for resisting control and reclaiming power. With humor and sharp insights, Stewart Reynolds shows how cat-like resilience, sass, and adaptability can arm us to face authoritarian nonsense head-on and demand our rights with fierce confidence, just like a feisty tabby claiming its food.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. The Gift of Not Belonging How Outsiders Thrive in a World of Joiners

    Rami Kaminski

    In "The Gift of Not Belonging", Rami Kaminski reveals that embracing your status as an outsider can be a powerful catalyst for self-discovery and success. By shedding the pressure to conform, you unlock fresh perspectives and opportunities that often elude those consumed by a need to belong. This thought-provoking guide shows you how to harness the unique strengths of your nonconformity to thrive in a world that often favors "joiners".

    Buy on Bookshop.org

Frequently asked questions

What kind of books are similar to The Irresistible Urge to Fall for Your Enemy?
Expect more contemporary romance novels with a focus on forbidden love, enemies-to-lovers tropes, and complex, often tumultuous relationships.
Are the recommended books just as emotionally intense as the original?
Yes, these reads feature similarly passionate and often heart-wrenching portrayals of love and rivalry.
Can I find any YA or fantasy options among these recommendations?
While the core focus is on contemporary romance, there are a few YA and fantasy elements sprinkled throughout the suggested titles, so feel free to explore those as well.
