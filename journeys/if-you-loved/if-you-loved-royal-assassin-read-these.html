If You Loved Royal Assassin, Read These

Immerse yourself in more epic, character-driven fantasies with complex protagonists and intricate worldbuilding.

Who these books are for

  • Fantasy readers seeking complex characters and immersive worldbuilding
  • Fans of Robin Hobb's Assassin's Trilogy
  • Readers who enjoy character-driven epic fantasy

Start here

If you only read one, choose Assassin's Quest by Robin Hobb for a thrilling continuation of Fitz's journey.

If you devoured every word of Robin Hobb's Royal Assassin, you're likely craving more epic fantasy with deep characters, intricate plots, and a dash of magic. These 10 novels share elements of the Assassin's Trilogy's allure, plunging readers into richly detailed worlds filled with complex protagonists, political intrigue, and high-stakes action. Whether you're seeking more sword and sorcery, morally ambiguous heroes, or immersive, character-driven storytelling, this curated list has something for you.

The collection

  1. Assassin's Quest

    Robin Hobb

    In the riveting final installment of Robin Hobb's Farseer Trilogy, Fitz, believed dead but living on in spirit, seeks bloody retribution against his treacherous half-brother Regal for the murder of their father, King Shrewd. This dark and mesmerizing tale of politics, power, and personal struggle will draw you in, as Fitz's epic journey tests the very limits of his Farseer heritage and magic. A must-read for any fan of fantasy and unforgettable characters in a richly crafted world.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. Warrior Princess Assassin A Novel

    Brigid Kemmerer

    In Brigid Kemmerer's addictive adult fantasy debut, interwoven fates and forbidden passions ignite amidst political intrigue and ancient magic. Meet Princess Jory, duty-bound to secure her kingdom's survival through a marriage of convenience with feared warrior King Maddox Kyronan. But their budding desires are threatened by Jory's secret: her family's magic is fading. Enter Asher, a former nobleman turned deadly assassin, whose job to kill Jory and Ky sets him on a collision course with his own heart. Will this trio navigate treacherous alliances, hidden deceptions, and their own increasingly irresistible attractions to save their realms and discover the true cost of love? With pulse-pounding action, lush world-building, and smoldering romance, Warrior Princess Assassin is your next obsession

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. The Man Who Died Twice A Thursday Murder Club Mystery

    Richard Osman

    Laugh-out-loud fun and clever mystery abound in this second installment of the Thursday Murder Club series from Richard Osman. Join Elizabeth, Joyce, Ron, and Ibrahim as they once again become amateur sleuths, this time dealing with a diamond heist and a ruthless killer. With their quick wit, charm, and experience, will the septuagenarian quartet catch the culprit before it's too late? Dive into this unputdownable, delightful read for an unforgettable detective adventure filled with humor and heart.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. A Good Girl's Guide to Murder

    Holly Jackson

    In this gripping page-turner, Pip sets out to uncover the truth behind her friend's supposed murder, revealing a tangled web of secrets in her small town. With each new twist, Pip's own life is put in danger, propelling her toward an unexpected ending. Perfect for mystery lovers and fans of complex, suspenseful teen thrillers.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. Assassin's Apprentice

    Robin Hobb

    "Diving into 'Assassin's Apprentice' is akin to embarking on a mystical journey, steeped in rich, evocative prose that transports you to a world of wonder and danger. As you witness young Fitz grow from an orphaned boy to a skilled assassin, the narrative weaves an emotional tapestry that tugs at the heartstrings and compels you to turn the page, craving more of this enchanting, hard-hitting fantasy."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. The Raven Scholar

    Antonia Hodgson

    In "The Raven Scholar", Antonia Hodgson crafts a mesmerizing Gothic tale woven from the dark threads of history and mystery. Set amidst the eerie halls of an 18th century Oxford college, this suspenseful thriller delves into the grim underbelly of academia, exploring themes of forbidden knowledge, tragic obsession, and the price of ambition. Hodgson's evocative prose and meticulous research transport readers into a haunting world where the boundaries between reality and madness blur, ensuring a riveting and unsettling literary journey.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. Heated Rivalry Now Streaming on Crave and HBO Max

    Rachel Reid

    In 'Heated Rivalry', Rachel Reid ignites a passionate and provocative tale that delves into the heady complexities of long-buried feelings, professional animosity, and the scorching heat of forbidden desire. As former teammates-turned-nemesis Luke and Tanner navigate a searing game of cat-and-mouse, Reid masterfully weaves together themes of trauma, queer identity, and the fierce push-and-pull of rivalry transformed into something far more intimate. This evocative and deeply human romance will leave readers breathless and yearning for more.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. Crown of Midnight

    Sarah J. Maas

    In Crown of Midnight, the thrilling sequel to Throne of Glass, Sarah J. Maas masterfully weaves a tale of deception, danger, and dark magic set in a treacherously divided kingdom. As the King's Champion, Celaena must navigate treacherous court politics while secretly seeking justice, her loyalties torn between rival factions. With the line between friend and foe blurred and a terrible tragedy looming, Celaena must confront the darkness within herself and decide what she's truly fight for in this epic, action-packed fantasy romance.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. The Heirs

    Faridah Àbíké-Íyímídé

    Within the labyrinthine Button Manor, five teen prodigies, each the master of their craft, must unravel the tangled threads of their adoptive father's murder. In this gripping whodunit, suspects lurk in every shadow, secrets simmer beneath the surface, and 17-year-olds Octavius, Fola, Bilal, Perdita, and Romeo must navigate the treacherous landscape of their own minds and each other's secrets to uncover the truth. With its witty banter, sharp wit, and intricate plot, this modern take on the classic murder mystery will keep you on the edge of your seat until the final stunning reveal. Perfect for fans of twisty YA thrillers like The Inheritance Games and Ace of Spades, The Heirs is a wild, addictive ride

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. Swordheart

    T. Kingfisher

    Immerse yourself in this whimsical fantasy romance where a sword's ancient magic unlocks a hilarious, heartwarming adventure. When Halla inherits a fortune and outwits greedy relatives, a mysterious immortal warrior named Sarkis appears, bound to protect her. Together, they fend off an array of absurd threats, growing closer with each laugh-filled escapade. But the biggest danger may be the enchanted sword itself. T. Kingfisher's deliciously charming tale combines the delightful wit of The Princess Bride with the steamy bodyguard romance of From Blood and Ash, making it an irresistible read.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

Frequently asked questions

What are good alternatives to Robin Hobb's Assassin's Trilogy?
Try sword and sorcery novels with morally complex protagonists, such as the Throne of Glass series by Sarah J. Maas or The First Law Trilogy by Joe Abercrombie.
Are there any fantasy books with assassin characters?
Yes, Heated Rivalry by Rachel Reid and Warrior Princess Assassin by Brigid Kemmerer feature strong assassin protagonists in their stories.
Can I find other epic fantasy series with a focus on politics and magic?
Absolutely! The Gentlemen Bastards series by Scott Lynch and The Malazan Book of the Fallen by Steven Erikson deliver richly detailed worldbuilding and complex politics, often with magical elements.
