If You Loved The Lost Book of Lancelot, Read These 8 Books

Immerse yourself in enchanting tales of lost worlds, mythical creatures, and epic quests with these captivating reads.

Who these books are for

  • Fantasy and adventure readers
  • Fans of Philip Pullman and J.R.R. Tolkien
  • Those seeking immersive, myth-rich stories

Start here

If you only read one, choose The Book of Dust: La Belle Sauvage for a richly detailed alternate universe.

The Lost Book of Lancelot's captivating blend of historical fiction, fantasy, and adventure has captivated readers. If you're seeking more immersive tales of lost worlds, mythical creatures, and epic quests, here are 8 novels that will transport you to enchanting realms.

The collection

  1. The Book of Dust: La Belle Sauvage (Book of Dust, Volume 1)

    Philip Pullman

    Step into the enchanting world of Philip Pullman's latest masterpiece, The Book of Dust: La Belle Sauvage, a stunning prequel to the beloved His Dark Materials trilogy. Follow Malcolm Polstead, an astute young boy, as he unravels the mysteries surrounding the enigmatic Lyra Belacqua, a girl destined for greatness. With its richly detailed setting, unforgettable characters, and Pullman's signature blend of fantasy and philosophical depth, this first volume of the Book of Dust trilogy is an unmissable adventure that will captivate readers of all ages.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  2. J.R.R. Tolkien 4-Book Boxed Set: The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings The Hobbit, The Fellowship of the Ring, The Two Towers, The Return of the King

    J.R.R. Tolkien

    Step into Middle-earth with this magnificent 4-volume set, featuring J.R.R. Tolkien's beloved classic, The Hobbit, and the epic high fantasy trilogy, The Lord of the Rings. Join Bilbo Baggins on a thrilling adventure as he discovers hidden depths of courage and wit, and follow the perilous quest of Frodo and his Fellowship as they battle to save their world from the dark lord Sauron's grasp. Immerse yourself in Tolkien's richly detailed world of myth and legend, where orcs, elves, dwarves, and hobbits collide in a timeless battle between good and darkness. This complete boxed set is a must-have for any fantasy fan.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. The Lost Spells

    Robert MacFarlane

    Rediscover the enchantment of nature with Robert MacFarlane's lyrical poetry and Jackie Morris's captivating illustrations in The Lost Spells. This poignant collection rekindles our relationship with the natural world, summoning vivid images of red foxes, birch trees, and jackdaws. A must-read for anyone seeking to re-wild their life and reminds us of the magic that slips away when we disconnect from the world around us.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. The Lost Bookshop

    Evie Woods

    Step into the enchanting world of The Lost Bookshop, where the magic of storytelling brings together three unlikely souls. Opaline, Martha, and Henry's ordinary lives are forever changed when they stumble upon a mysterious Dublin bookshop that seems to vanish and reappear at will. As they unravel the secrets hidden within its shelves, they become embroiled in a captivating tale that blurs the lines between reality and fantasy. With its beautifully woven prose and nods to classic literature, this charming novel is a must-read for book lovers everywhere, promising to sweep you away on a journey of self-discovery and the transformative power of reading.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. The First Time I Saw Him

    Laura Dave

    In this captivating follow-up to The Last Thing He Told Me, Laura Dave masterfully weaves another spellbinding tale, this time delving into themes of forgiveness and the enduring power of love. Picking up where the previous story left off, The First Time I Saw Him promises an equally gripping, rich, and evocative reading experience, sure to leave readers eagerly turning the pages to unravel the next chapter in this unforgettable saga.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. His Dark Materials: The Golden Compass (Book 1)

    Philip Pullman

    Join 11-year-old Lyra on a thrilling quest through magical realms as she uncovers a sinister plot to exploit innocent children. With her courage, wit, and loyal daemon by her side, Lyra must navigate treacherous landscapes, outwit foes, and make heart-wrenching choices between friends and worlds, all while unraveling the secrets of a mysterious substance called Dust. This first installment of the beloved His Dark Materials trilogy is a masterful blend of fantasy, adventure, and philosophical intrigue that will captivate readers young and old.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. Lost and Found Jigsaw Book

    Oliver Jeffers

    Indulge in a heartwarming tale of unlikely friendship as a curious boy and an adventurous penguin navigate the ups and downs of companionship. With Oliver Jeffers' endearing storytelling and delightful illustrations, this charming picture book is a must-read for kids of all ages. Now, take the immersive experience to another level by completing the six included 12-piece jigsaw puzzles, bringing each whimsical scene to vivid life.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. Swann's Way In Search of Lost Time, Volume 1 (Penguin Classics Deluxe Edition)

    Marcel Proust

    Step into the enchanting world of Marcel Proust's Swann's Way, the first volume of his transcendent In Search of Lost Time. With Lydia Davis's award-winning translation, rediscover childhood memories and impressions that vividly come to life, as seen through the eyes of a sensitive young narrator. Also featured is the iconic novella "Swann in Love", a profound exploration of jealousy that intertwines to form the sprawling masterpiece of Proust's life in art. Dive into this timeless work of literary fiction, a perfect blend of satire, skepticism, and introspection that renders the passage of time in exquisite detail.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. The Phantom Tollbooth

    Norton Juster

    Step through the Tollbooth and discover a world where words come alive and even the most mundane day can be transformed into an unforgettable adventure. Join Milo on his whimsical journey as he outwits the watchdog Tock, rescues Rhyme and Reason, and learns that the blandest existence can hold the most exciting secrets. With Jules Feiffer's iconic illustrations illuminating Norton Juster's clever wordplay, The Phantom Tollbooth is a timeless classic that will charm and delight generations of readers.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. The Way of Kings Book One of the Stormlight Archive

    Brandon Sanderson

    "Step into the sweeping epic of 'The Way of Kings', where ancient magic echoes in the wind and destinies crisscross across a realm on the cusp of revolution. Amidst a world of towering Stormlight-wielding warriors and enigmatic mystics, Sanderson masterfully weaves together threads of power, tradition, and the eternal struggle between order and chaos. Prepare to be enthralled by a tale as vast and intricate as the world itself, where even the smallest choice can reshape the course of history."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

Frequently asked questions

What other fantasy series feature complex world-building?
The Malazan Book of the Fallen by Steven Erikson, The First Law Trilogy by Joe Abercrombie, and The Kingkiller Chronicle by Patrick Rothfuss are notable examples.
Can I find books with mythological elements?
Yes, try The Golem and the Jinni by Helene Wecker or The Poppy War by R.F. Kuang for unique mythological blends.
Are there any standalone novels like The Lost Book of Lancelot?
Yes, consider The Buried Giant by Kazuo Ishiguro or The Night Circus by Erin Morgenstern for enchanting, self-contained tales
