Bird Guides & Birding Accessories

For bird enthusiasts who loved David Sibley's Flashcards, explore more of his comprehensive guides, postcards, and interactive puzzle books for a deeper connection with nature's feathered friends.

Who these books are for

  • Birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts
  • Backyard birding hobbyists
  • Beginners and experienced ornithologists alike

Start here

If you only read one, choose Sibley's Field Guide to Birds of Eastern North America for its unparalleled detail and accuracy.

David Sibley's Sibley Backyard Birding Flashcards, Revised and Updated, has delighted bird enthusiasts with its concise, user-friendly format. For those seeking more in-depth knowledge or fun, interactive ways to engage with birds, explore these recommended titles. From comprehensive field guides to postcards, trivia games, and puzzles, these books provide a variety of ways to deepen your understanding and appreciation of the avian world.

Frequently asked questions

What if I'm not in the Eastern region?
Sibley also offers guides for Western and Complete North American bird species.
Are these books suitable for children?
Many titles, especially the postcards and puzzle books, can engage kids and spark an interest in birds.
What if I prefer digital resources?
Sibley offers a range of mobile apps and eBooks for convenient bird identification on-the-go.
