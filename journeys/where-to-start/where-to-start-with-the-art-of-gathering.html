Where to Start with The Art of Gathering

For a foundational understanding, begin with Priya Parker's self-help book 'The Art of Gathering: How We Meet and Why It Matters'. It provides a clear introduction to hosting gatherings that foster meaningful connections.

Who these books are for

  • Event planners
  • Hosts and hostsesses
  • Party throwers
  • Meeting organizers
  • Social butterflies
  • Anyone looking to build stronger connections

Start here

If you only read one, choose 'The Art of Gathering' as it sets the stage for a deeper understanding of the art of hosting gatherings.

In today's fast-paced world, meaningful connections and memorable experiences have become a rare commodity. Enter 'The Art of Gathering' by Priya Parker, a thought-provoking guide that delves into the art and science of hosting gatherings that leave a lasting impact. By understanding the principles of effective hosting, you can transform any gathering into an unforgettable event that fosters deeper relationships and a stronger sense of community.

Frequently asked questions

Is 'The Art of Gathering' a comprehensive guide to hosting events?
While a great starting point, it focuses more on the principles rather than extensive recipes for specific occasions.
Can I apply the concepts to small, everyday gatherings or just large events?
The principles are universal and applicable to any size or type of gathering.
Does the book provide actionable advice?
Yes, Parker shares numerous practical tips and stories to illustrate her ideas in a relatable and engaging way.
