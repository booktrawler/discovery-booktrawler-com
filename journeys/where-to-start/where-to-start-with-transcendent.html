Where to Start with Transcendent

Begin with Laverne Cox's memoir 'Transcendent', a powerful and intimate exploration of identity, fame, and the search for authenticity.

Who these books are for

  • New readers of transgender experiences and memoirs
  • Fans of Laverne Cox and her work
  • Anyone interested in identity, authenticity, and resilience

Start here

If you only read one, choose Laverne Cox's 'Transcendent' for its unflinching honesty and inspiring story of self-discovery.

Laverne Cox's 'Transcendent' is a landmark memoir that provides an unflinching look at the experiences of a transgender woman in the public eye. With its lyrical prose and raw honesty, this book offers a powerful entry point for readers new to transgender narratives while also resonating with those familiar with the challenges and triumphs of self-discovery. Whether you're a fan of Laverne Cox or simply interested in exploring complex themes of identity and authenticity, 'Transcendent' is an essential read for anyone seeking a more nuanced understanding of the human condition.

The collection

  1. Transcendent A Memoir

    Laverne Cox

    In her deeply personal memoir Transcendent, Laverne Cox shares a vulnerable and powerful account of her journey as a transgender woman in Hollywood, from overcoming childhood trauma and self-doubt to becoming a groundbreaking star and unstoppable advocate for LGBTQ+ rights. With unflinching honesty, Cox illuminates the highs and lows of her career, grappling with identity, body image, and loneliness in a world often hostile to her existence. This is a transformative and necessary book that will resonate with anyone seeking courage, resilience, and a renewed sense of purpose in the face of adversity.

  2. The Beginning Comes After the End Notes on a World of Change

    Rebecca Solnit

    Experience the thrilling transformation of our world over the past 60 years with renowned author Rebecca Solnit in her New York Times bestseller, The Beginning Comes After the End. Spanning politics, spirituality, and culture, Solnit reveals how despite pushback, radical change is inevitable as we dismantle the old and build a new civilization embracing interconnection, antiracism, and environmental stewardship.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  3. Gender Rituals Creating a Sacred Space for Your Body, Your Gender, and Your Becoming

    Eli Lawliet PhD

    Embark on a transformative journey of self-discovery and empowerment with Gender Rituals. As a pioneering gender doula and trans expert, Eli Lawliet guides you through sacred practices to reconnect with your body, honor your gender identity, and cultivate trans joy. Whether you're just beginning to explore your gender or celebrating milestones, this inclusive and compassionate guide helps you reclaim your journey as profound, affirming, and valuable – no matter how you identify.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  4. Hell Followed with Us

    Andrew Joseph White

    "Join Benji, a fierce trans teenager, in his fight against the apocalyptic cult that raised him and now seeks to exploit his mutated powers. Finding refuge with a group of LGBTQ+ teens at the Acheson Center, Benji must learn to control his deadly new abilities while uncovering the leader's secrets. A thrilling, queer debut filled with action, heart, and a defiant stance against the monsters of oppression."

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  5. The Vanishing Half A Novel

    Brit Bennett

    In "The Vanishing Half", Brit Bennett crafts a riveting tale of twin sisters who, propelled by desperation and identity crises, literally disappear into each other in a stunning act of duality. As their lives unfold, Bennett probes the complex intersections of race, class, and the human condition, weaving a mesmerizing narrative that transcends time and place. With lyrical prose and a keen eye for the nuances of the Caribbean-American experience, this haunting novel immerses readers in a world of longing, love, and the unyielding power of our essential selves.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  6. Transitions (40th Anniversary Edition) Making Sense of Life's Changes

    William Bridges

    Discover the timeless wisdom of navigating life's inevitable changes with William Bridges' seminal guide, now in a 40th anniversary edition. Named a top 50 self-help book of all time, Transitions offers a step-by-step roadmap to master the three stages of transition: endings, the neutral zone, and new beginnings. This accessible, insightful work empowers readers to make sense of change and emerge stronger, more hopeful, and ready for what's next.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  7. Wild Reverence A Novel

    Rebecca Ross

    Join Rebecca Ross on a captivating journey through realms of gods and mortals in the lush, luxe edition of Wild Reverence. This beautifully designed novel follows Matilda, a young goddess hiding secrets beneath her messenger magic, and Vincent, a hardened mortal lord who shares a fated connection with her. As their paths converge, Matilda must confront her deepest fears to rewrite the brutal ways of the gods and risk everything for love. This sweeping tale of tension, romance, and dark secrets will enthrall you.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  8. The Hyphenated Life Bridging the In-Between Spaces of Intersectional Identities

    Han Ren

    In this eye-opening guide, Dr. Han Ren, a licensed psychologist and herself a hyphenate, offers invaluable insights and practical strategies for those living with intersectional identities. Whether you are a child of immigrants, disabled, neurodivergent, queer, or navigating multiple marginalized spaces, "The Hyphenated Life" empowers you to understand and express your multifaceted self fully, no matter the environment. A must-read for anyone seeking to bridge the gaps of identity and belong, while staying true to your unique experience.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  9. Worthy of the Event An Essay

    Vivian Blaxell

    Embark on a dazzling, genre-bending journey with Vivian Blaxell's Worthy of the Event, a prize-winning literary masterpiece that defies categorization. This expansive essay weaves together the author's personal transgender experience in 1960s Australia with philosophical musings, historical vignettes, and explorations of Indigenous wisdom and animal consciousness. Blaxell's irreverent wit and profound insights illuminate the human condition in ways both exhilarating and unsettling, cementing her status as a major literary voice of her generation. Prepare to be transported and transformed by this singular, unforgettable work.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

  10. Annihilation A Novel (10th Anniversary Edition)

    Jeff VanderMeer

    Dive into the unsettling world of Area X, a realm where nature has consumed the remnants of human civilization, in Jeff VanderMeer's gripping debut novel, Annihilation. Join a team of four women, each with their own motivations and secrets, as they venture into the unknown, confronting lifeforms that defy explanation and unravel the mysteries that lurk within themselves. This critically-acclaimed, Nebula Award-winning tale weaves together elements of science fiction, horror, and literary fiction to create a thought-provoking and deeply unsettling journey that will leave readers questioning the very boundaries of reality.

    Buy on Bookshop.org

Frequently asked questions

Is 'Transcendent' suitable for all ages?
While the book explores mature themes, it's suitable for readers 16 and up.
Are there other books like 'Transcendent' that deal with transgender issues?
Yes, check out 'The Vanishing Half' by Brit Bennett and 'Annihilation' by Jeff VanderMeer for thought-provoking explorations of identity and self-discovery.
What other genres relate to the themes in 'Transcendent'?
Memoirs, coming-of-age stories, and essays often touch on identity, self-discovery, and the human condition, making them great companions to 'Transcendent'.
