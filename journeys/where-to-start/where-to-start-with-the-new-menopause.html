Where to Start with The New Menopause

For a comprehensive introduction, begin with Dr. Jen Gunter's The Menopause Manifesto, which demystifies the topic with scientific accuracy and personal candor.

Who these books are for

  • Women experiencing menopause or perimenopause
  • Healthcare providers seeking menopause educational resources
  • Individuals interested in women's health and wellness

Start here

If you only read one, choose The Menopause Manifesto for its insightful, accessible take on the subject.

The menopause experience has evolved significantly, with increasing awareness of its diverse impacts on physical, emotional, and social well-being. This reading journey provides an essential starting point for navigating the wealth of contemporary menopause literature, guiding readers to the most informative and engaging books available today.

Frequently asked questions

Is The Menopause Manifesto only for women?
No, while primarily focused on women, it offers valuable insights for anyone supporting a woman through menopause.
Will it cover the full range of menopause-related topics?
Yes, it addresses physical symptoms, medical treatments, and emotional aspects of menopause in a thorough yet engaging manner.
Is Dr. Gunter's book suitable for a beginner?
Absolutely – it assumes no prior medical knowledge and uses clear, approachable language to explain complex concepts.
