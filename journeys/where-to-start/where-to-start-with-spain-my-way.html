Where to Start with Spain My Way

Begin with José Andrés' Spain My Way to embark on a culinary journey through the country's diverse regions and flavors.

Who these books are for

  • Food and travel enthusiasts
  • Those interested in Spanish culture and cuisine

Start here

If you only read one, choose Spain My Way for an authentic taste of the country's rich gastronomic heritage.

Embark on an epicurean adventure through Spain with José Andrés' memoir Spain My Way. This engaging book takes you on a gastronomic journey across the country's diverse regions, from the seafood-rich Cantabrian coast to the sun-kissed olive groves of Andalusia. Along the way, Andrés shares personal anecdotes, historical context, and mouth-watering recipes that capture the essence of Spanish cuisine and culture.

Frequently asked questions

What kind of books does Spain My Way belong to?
It falls under the categories of travel, cookbooks, and food memoirs.
Can I learn about Spain beyond food with this book?
While the focus is on cuisine, the author shares insights into Spanish culture, history, and daily life along the way.
Is the book suitable for all ages?
While appealing to adults, the engaging storytelling and vivid descriptions make it appealing for younger readers with an interest in food and travel as well.
