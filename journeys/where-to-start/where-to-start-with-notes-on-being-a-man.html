Where to Start with Notes on Being a Man

Begin with Scott Galloway's Notes on Being a Man, a provocative and insightful exploration of masculinity in modern society.

Who these books are for

  • Men seeking a deeper understanding of modern masculinity
  • Women interested in the evolution of male roles and expectations
  • Individuals looking for a thought-provoking introduction to gender studies

Start here

If you only read one book on the subject, make it Scott Galloway's Notes on Being a Man, as it provides a comprehensive and engaging foundation.

In Notes on Being a Man, Scott Galloway presents a refreshingly honest and insightful exploration of masculinity in the 21st century. Through a series of engaging essays, he delves into the complexities and contradictions of being a man today, from navigating relationships and personal growth to grappling with privilege and societal expectations. This groundbreaking work offers a much-needed conversation starter for anyone seeking a deeper understanding of modern masculinity and its implications for individuals and society as a whole.

Frequently asked questions

What is Notes on Being a Man about?
The book examines the challenges and complexities of being a man in today's world, covering topics like male identity, privilege, and relationships.
Is Notes on Being a Man a memoir or self-help book?
No, it's a collection of essays that offer personal anecdotes and reflections, but also delve into broader cultural and philosophical discussions.
How does Notes on Being a Man contribute to the conversation about masculinity?
By providing a candid, humorous, and often critical perspective, Galloway helps redefine traditional notions of masculinity and encourages readers to rethink their roles and expectations.
