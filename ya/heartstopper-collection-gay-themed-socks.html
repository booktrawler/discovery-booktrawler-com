Delight in the charming world of Nick and Charlie with these Heartstopper books and matching socks, perfect for fans of lighthearted LGBTQ+ stories and humorous coming-of-age tales.

Start here

If you only read one Heartstopper book, choose 'Heartstopper, Volume 1' to meet Nick and Charlie and embark on their heartwarming journey.

Step into the delightful world of Nick and Charlie with the Heartstopper series and official merchandise. This charming, humorous, and heartwarming collection follows the blossoming romance and friendship between two teenage boys at a British boarding school. With graphic novels, matching socks, and an uplifting message of love and acceptance, Heartstopper is a must-read for fans of lighthearted LGBTQ+ stories and relatable coming-of-age tales.

Frequently asked questions

What is the Heartstopper series about?
Heartstopper is a charming, humorous, and heartwarming graphic novel series by Alice Oseman that follows the blossoming romance and friendship between Nick and Charlie, two teenage boys at a British boarding school.
Are the socks official merchandise?
Yes, the Heartstopper socks are officially licensed merchandise, matching the iconic designs from the graphic novels.
Can I gift these books and socks?
Absolutely! The Heartstopper collection makes a thoughtful, uplifting gift for friends, family, or yourself – perfect for any occasion.
Are these books suitable for younger readers?
While the themes are appropriate for all ages, some mature humor and teenage angst may not appeal to the very youngest readers. Parental discretion advised.
