Who these books are for

Frequently asked questions

What are autistic burnout and its signs?
Burnout is a state of emotional, mental, and physical exhaustion caused by chronic stress, overstimulation, and unmet needs. Common signs include social withdrawal, decreased productivity, and increased irritability.
Are these books only for autistic adults?
While designed primarily for autistic individuals, these titles can also be helpful for family members, caregivers, and professionals seeking to better understand and support autistic people.
Do I need a clinical background to benefit from these books?
No, these books are written in accessible language and don't require a medical or psychological education to understand and apply the concepts and strategies.
Are there fiction books about autism that I should know about?
Yes, there are many excellent fiction novels that portray authentic autistic experiences. Some notable examples include 'The Reason I Jump' memoir by Naoki Higashida, 'The A List' by J.C. Burke, and 'Sawkill Girls' by Claire Legrand.
How can I find more books about autism and neurodivergence?
Explore online catalogs like Goodreads, browse bookstores' neurodiversity sections, and follow publishers, authors, and advocates on social media to discover new titles and stay updated on the latest releases.
